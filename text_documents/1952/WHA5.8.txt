WHA5.8  Budget  Level  for 1953 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the  budget  level  for 1953  should  be US  $9,832,754,  to be financed  by : 
(1) casual  income  available  for  1953  of $852,554  ; 
(2) assessments  against  all Members  of $8,980,200. 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed., 2.1 (First  report  of the  Committee  on  Programme  and 
Budget,  adopted  at the  seventh  plenary  meeting, 12 May  1952)
