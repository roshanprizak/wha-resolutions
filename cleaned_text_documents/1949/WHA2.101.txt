VII The Second World Health Assembly, Having reconsidered the proposed substitution of the words " involves or would involve " for the words " would involve in the Agreement between the International Labour Organization and the World Health Organization ; 107 Noting that the substitution would make little difference to the Agreement, and would probably require similar amendments toa number of other agreements to which ILO is a party, DECIDES not to urge the proposed alteration of Article VII. (Sixth report of Committee on Constitutional Matters, adopted at tenth plenary meeting)
