WHA25.11  Annual  Report  of the Director-General  for 1971 
The Twenty-fifth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  reviewed  the  report  of the  Director-General  on the  work  of the World  Health  Organization 
during  1971，1 
1. NOTES  with  satisfaction  the manner  in which  the programme  was  planned  and  carried  out  in 1971,  in 
accordance  with  the established  policies  of the Organization;  and 
2. COMMENDS  the Director-General  for  the work  accomplished. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed.’ 1.16.1 Ninth  plenary  meeting,  17  May  1972
