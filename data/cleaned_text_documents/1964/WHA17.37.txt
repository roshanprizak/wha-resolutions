reThe Seventeenth World Health Assembly, Noting resolution EB33.R22 of the Executive Board on the subject of the establishment of a World Health Research Centre; and Considering the desirability of applying the latest advances in the science and technology of communications under the aegis of WHO towards improving and co-ordinating the world-wide exchange of information on health problems and biomedical research, 1.
