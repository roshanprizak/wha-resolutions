The Second World Health Assembly (1) NOTES the resolution proposed by the Italian delegation concerning the transformation of the Expert Committee on Malaria into an expert committee on malaria and other insect-borne diseases (2) NOTES the comments thereon expressed by the delegation of the Philippines (3) RESOLVES that this question be referred to the Executive Board for consideration with a request to report on the subject to the Third World Health Assembly. (Sixth report of Committee on Programme, adopted at tenth plenary meeting)
