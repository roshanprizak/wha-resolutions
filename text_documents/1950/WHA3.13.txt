WHA3.13  Expert  Committee  on Antibiotics  : Report  on the First  Session 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the report  of the Expert  Committee  on Antibiotics  on its first  session  14 and the 
resolution  taken  on it by the ad hoc  committee  of the Executive  Board,15 
1. NOTES  the report  of the expert  committee  ; 
2. THANKS  the experts  for their  work  ; 
3. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to examine  the report  from  the point  of view  of the practicability  of 
the recommendations  therein,  in the light  of the discussions  which  have  taken  place  in the Committee  on 
Programme,  and  to take  any  desirable  action  within  the budgetary  resources  available  ; 
4. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to consider  the desirability  of asking  the expert  committee  to examine  the 
feasibility  of : 
(1) drawing  up a concrete  programme  for the training  of experts  in the preparation  of antibiotics  and 
in research  thereon, 
(2) organizing  such  training, 
(3) setting  up, under  the supervision  of WHO,  a pilot  plant  for the production  of antibiotics  and  for 
research  on that  subject, 
(4) collecting  and  making  available  to interested  health  administrations  precise  information  on the 
cost of construction  and  operation  of an antibiotic  plant,  including  equipment  and  material  required 
and personnel  needed  for effective  operation. 
(Third  report  of the Committee  on Programme,  adopted 
at sixth  plenary  meeting,  19 May  1950)  [A3/R/29]
