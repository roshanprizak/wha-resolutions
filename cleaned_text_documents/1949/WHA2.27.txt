The Second World Health Assembly (1) NOTES the report of the Director-General on the study of the supply of insulin which shows that present and future world supplies of insulin are adequate in quantity and quality to meet the normal requirements, and (2) REQUESTS the Director-General to advise governments, upon request, concerning the means of obtaining the necessary requirements of insulin and to explore the possibilities of manufacture in various countries. (Eighth report of Committee on Programme, adopted at tenth plenary meeting)
