The Twenty-fourth World Health Assembly, Recalling resolution WHA23.26; and Having considered the recommendations made by the Executive Board in its resolution EB47.R48, 1. DCIDS that the study on medical literature services to Members should be continued for another year; and 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board to report on its study to the Twenty-fifth World Health Assembly. Handb. Res., 11th ed., 7.4 Sixteenth plenary meeting, 18 May 1971 (Committee B, fourth report)
