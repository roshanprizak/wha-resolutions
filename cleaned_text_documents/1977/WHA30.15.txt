The Thirtieth World Health Assembly 1. RESOLVES that Mr Sven-Ivar Ivarsson be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the two financial years 1978 and 1979 and that he make his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations, with the provision that, should the necessity arisehe may designate a representative to actin his absence 2. EXPRESSES its appreciation to Mr Lindmark for the services rendered to the Organization during the years in which he has served as its External Auditor. Handb.
