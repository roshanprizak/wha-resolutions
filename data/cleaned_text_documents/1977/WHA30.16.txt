The Thirtieth World Health Assembly, Recalling resolution WHA29.33; Having considered the recommendation made by the Executive Board in its resolution EB59.R33; 1. DECIDES that the study on WHO's role at the country level, particularly the role of the WHO representatives, should be continued for another year; 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board to report on its study to the Thirty-first World Health Assembly. Handb. Res., Vol. II (2nd ed.)7.4 Tenth plenary meeting, 12 May 1977 (Committee B, third report)
