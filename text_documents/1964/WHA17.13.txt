WHA17.13  Addition  to Schedule  A to the Appropriation  Resolution  for  the Financial  Year  1964 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly 
DECIDES  that,  since  Kenya,  Northern  Rhodesia,  Nyasaland  and  Zanzibar  are  carrying  out  malaria 
programmes,  they  are  eligible  for  credits  in 1964  in the  same  way  as those  Members  listed  in Schedule  A 
attached  to the  Appropriation  Resolution  for  the  financial  year  1964.  1 
Handb.  Res.,  7th ed.,  2.1.17 Seventh  plenary  meeting,  10  March  1964  (Committee
