WHA5.49  Duration  of Sessions  of the Health  Assembly 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to carry  out,  in conjunction  with  the Director-General,  an examination 
of the work  of the Health  Assembly  with  a view  to the completion  of Health  Assemblies  within  a substantially  shorter  period  than  at present. 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed., 4.1 (Fourth  report  of the Committee  on  Programme  and Budget,  adopted  at  the  ninth  plenary  meeting, 21 May  1952)
