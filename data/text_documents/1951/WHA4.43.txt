WHA4.43  Admission  to Membership  of the World  Health  Organization  : Federal  Republic  of Germany 
The Fourth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  the Federal  Republic  of Germany  as a Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject 
to the deposit  of an instrument  of acceptance  of the Constitution  in accordance  with  Article  79. 
(First  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  seventh 
plenary  meeting,  16  May  1951)  [A4/R/13]
