ADMISSION  OF A NEW MEMBER；  COOK ISLANDS 
The Thirty-seventh  World Health Assembly 
ADMITS the Cook Islands  as a Member of the World Health Organization,  subject  to the 
deposit of a formal instrument  with the Secretary-General  of the United Nations  in accordance 
with Article  79 of the Constitution.
