The Forty-fifth World Health Assembly, Noting the recommendations of the Executive Board with regard to remuneration of staff in the ungraded posts and of the Director-General, 1. ESTABLISHES the salary for the posts of Assistant Directors-General and Regional Directors at US$ 124 560 per annum before staff assessment, resulting in a modified net salary of US$ 74 571 (dependency rate) or US$ 67 436 (single rate); 2. ESTABLISHES the salary for the post of Deputy Director-General at US$ 139 417 per annum before staff assessment, resulting in a modified net salary of US$ 82 297 (dependency rate) or US$ 73 824 (single rate); 3. ESTABLISHES the salary for the Director-General at US$ 170 098 per annum before staff assessment, resulting in a modified net salary of US$ 98 251 (dependency rate) or US$ 87 017 (single rate); 4. DECIDES that these adjustments in remuneration shall be effective from 1 March 1992. Twelfth plenary meeting, 13 May 1992 A45 / VR / 12 i
