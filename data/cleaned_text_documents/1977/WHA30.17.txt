The Thirtieth World Health Assembly, Having considered the recommendation of the Executive Board in resolution EB59.R34 on the subject of the next organizational study, 1. DECIDES that the next subject of study shall be The role of WHO expert advisory panels and committees and collaborating centres in meeting the needs of WHO regarding expert advice and in carrying out technical activities of WHO"; 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board to report to the Thirty-second World Health Assembly on the study. Handb. Res., Vol. II (2nd ed.), 7.4 Tenth plenary meeting, 12 May 1977 (Committee B, third report)
