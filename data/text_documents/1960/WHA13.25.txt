WHA13.25  Accommodation  for the Regional  Office  for South-East  Asia 
The Thirteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  reports  of the Executive  Board  and  the  Director-General  on developments 
with  regard  to the accommodation  for the Regional  Office  for South-East  Asia,1 
1. NOTES  the progress  made  to date  toward  the construction  of the new  building;  and 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to report  developments  to the next  session  of the Executive  Board. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th  ed.,  5.2.3.2  Eighth  plenary  meeting,  11 May  1960  (section  13 of the second  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
