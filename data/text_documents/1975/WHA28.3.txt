WHA28.3  Admission  of a new  Member  : Mozambique 
The Twenty-eighth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Mozambique  as a Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  this  admission  to have  effect  from 
the date  of attainment  of independence  by Mozambique,  to take  place  on 25 June  1975，and  subject  to the 
deposit  of a formal  instrument  with  the Secretary-General  of the United  Nations  in accordance  with  Article  79 
of the Constitution. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  II，6.2.1.1 Fourth  plenary  meeting,  14 May  1975
