The Sixteenth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that Mr Uno Brunskog be appointed external auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the three financial years 1964 to 1966 inclusive, to make his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations, with the provision that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to actin his absence. Handb. Res., 6th ed., 7.1.11.1 Eleventh plenary meeting, 18 May 1963 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, second report)
