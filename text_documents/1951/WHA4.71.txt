WHA4.71  Budget  Level  for  1952 
The Fourth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the budget  level  for 1952  should  be $9,077,782,  to be financed  by : 
(1) casual  income  available  for 1952  of $477,782, 
(2) assessments  against  all Members  of $8,600,000. 
(First  report  of the  Joint  Meetings,  adopted  at the ninth  plenary  meeting,  19  May  1951)  [A4/R/18]
