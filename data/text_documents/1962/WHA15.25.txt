WHA15.25 Admission  of New  Associate  Members:  Jamaica 
The Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Jamaica  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice  being 
given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Jamaica  in accordance  with  Rules  111  and  112 
of the  Rules  of Procedure  of the  World  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th ed., 6.2.1.2  Tenth  plenary  meeting,  21 May  1962  (section  2 of the fifth  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
