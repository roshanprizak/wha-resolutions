12 Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a Person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
After  consideration  of the nominations  of the General  Committee,14 
ELECTS  the following  Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board  : 
Austria,  Costa  Rica,  Indonesia,  Iraq,  Switzerland  and  the United  States  of America. 
(Adopted  at the eighth  plenary  meeting,  15 May  1953)
