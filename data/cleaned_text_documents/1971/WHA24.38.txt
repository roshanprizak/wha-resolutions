The Twenty-fourth World Health Assembly, Having considered the recommendation of the Executive Board, 1. DECIDES that the subject of the organizational study tobe presented to the Twenty-sixth World Health Assembly shall be " Methods of promoting the development of basic health services "; and 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board to report on that study to the Twenty-sixth World Health Assembly. Handb. Res., 11th ed., 7.4 Sixteenth plenary meeting, 18 May 1971 (Committee B, fourth report)
