WHA17.42  Committee  on International  Quarantine  : Twelfth  Report 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  twelfth  report  of the  Committee  on International  Quarantine;  1 and 
Considering  that,  because  of the  date  of the  meeting  of the  Committee  on  International  Quarantine, 
Member  States  had  not  had  sufficient  time  to consider  the  report, 
1. THANKS  the members  of the  Committee  for  their  work; 
2. DEFERS  consideration  of the  proposed  amendments  to Appendix  4 of the  International  Sanitary  Regulations  (International  Certificate  of Vaccination  or Revaccination  against  Smallpox); 
3. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to obtain  the  observations  of Member  States  on the  proposed  amendments  and  to report  thereon  to the  Eighteenth  World  Health  Assembly;  and 
4. ADOPTS  the  twelfth  report  of the  Committee  on  International  Quarantine,  with  the  reservation  mentioned  in paragraph  2 of this  resolution. 
Handb  Res.,  7th ed.,  1.5.8.4 Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  19 March  1964  (Committee 
on Programme  and  Budget,  sixth  report)
