WHA8.31  Approved  1955  Technical  Assistance  Programme  and  the Financial  Situation  for 1955 
The Eighth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  a report  of the  Director-General  on the  Expanded  Programme  of Technical 
Assistance  for  1955,  and  on the  financial  situation  for  that  year,1  and 
Having  considered  resolution  EB15.R42  of the  Executive  Board  concerning  the  programme  for 
1955, 
1. CONCURS  in the opinions  expressed  by the Executive  Board  in that  resolution  ; and 
2. NOTES  that  the  Technical  Assistance  Board  has,  to date,  allocated  to WHO  an amount  of some 
$4 500 000 for approved  projects,  indirect  project  costs  and  central  administrative  costs  in 1955. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd ed., 3 Ninth  plenary  meeting,  26 May  1955  (section  2 of 
the third  report  of the Committee  on Programme  and 
Budget)
