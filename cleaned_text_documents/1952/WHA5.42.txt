The Fifth World Health Assembly, Having considered the rights and obligations of Associate Members; and Taking into account the admission of two additional Associate Members by the Fifth World Health Assembly REQUESTS the Executive Board to study this matter and submit a report to the Sixth World Health Assembly. Handb. Res., 1st d., 6.3.2.1 (Third report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the tenth plenary meeting, 21 May 1952)
