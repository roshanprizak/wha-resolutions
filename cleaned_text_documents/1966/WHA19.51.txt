The Nineteenth World Health Assembly, Considering resolution EB37.R8 adopted by the Executive Board at its thirty-seventh session; and Considering the great value and importance of the reports of the expert committees of the World Health Organization, SUGGESTS that Member States ensure that wide dissemination be given to recommendations of expert committees through reference toa national expert panel, or otherwise, so that the best use maybe made of such recommendations in the context of the development of their national health programmes. Handb. Res., 8th ed., 1.13.2 Fourteenth plenary meeting, 20 May 1966 (Committee on Programme and Budget, seventh report)
