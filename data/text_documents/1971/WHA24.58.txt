WHA24.58 General  programme  of work  covering  a specific  period  : 1973-1977 
The Twenty-fourth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  Article  28(g)  of the  Constitution; 
Having  reviewed  the general  programme  of work  for the specific  period  1973-1977  inclusive,  as prepared 
by the Executive  Board  at its forty-seventh  session;  1 and 
Believing  that  the  programme  provides  an adequate  broad  policy  framework  for the formulation  of 
annual  programmes  within  that  period, 
APPROVES  the general  programme  of work  for the specific  period  1973-1977  inclusive,  as prepared  by the 
Executive  Board  at its forty-seventh  session. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 1.1.1.3 Seventeenth  plenary  meeting,  20 May  1971 
(Committee  B,  sixth  report)
