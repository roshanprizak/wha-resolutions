The Twenty-eighth World Health Assembly 1. NOTES the reports of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Committee;2 2. ENDORSES the proposal of the Committee for the award of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Medal and Prize for 1975; 3. AWARDS the Medal and Prize to Dr G. R. Roashan; and 4. PAYS TRIBUTE to Dr G. R. Roashan for his most significant contribution to public health in the geographical area in which Dr A. T. Shousha served the World Health Organization. Handb. Res., Vol. II 9.1.3 Eleventh plenary meeting, 22 May 1975
