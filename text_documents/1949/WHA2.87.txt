WHA2.87.  Relations  with  the Specialized  Agencies  of the United  Nations 
The Second  World  Health  Assembly 
(1) EXPRESSES  its satisfaction  with  the  work  achieved  by WHO  during  the  past  year 
in its relations  with  the  specialized  agencies  of the  United  Nations  ; and 
(2) REQUESTS  that  full  co-operation  in this  field  should  be continued,  with  particular 
attention  to the further  development  of such  co-operation  at the  secretariat  level  during 
the planning  stage. 
(Third  report  of Committee  on Constitutional  Matters,  adopted  at ninth 
plenary  meeting)
