WHA3.76  Admission  of Viet  Nam,  Cambodia  and  Laos  to Membership  of WHO 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  the State  of Viet  Nam  and  the Kingdoms  of Cambodia  and  Laos  as Members  of the World Health  Organization,  subject  to the  deposit  of their  instruments  of acceptance  of the  Constitution  in accordance  with  Article  79. 
(Second  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at fifth  plenary meeting,  16 May  1950)  [A3/R/10]
