THIRTY-NINTH  WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY WHA39.1 
Supplementary  agenda item 1 12 May 1986 
ASSIGNMENT  OF MOROCCO  TO THE EASTERM  MEDITERANNEAN  REGION 
The Thirty-ninth  World Health Assembly, 
Having considered  the request  from the Govermment  of Morocco  for the inclusion  of that 
country in the Eastern  Mediterranean  Region; 
RESOLVES  that Morocco  shall form part of the Eastern  Mediterranean  Region. 
Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  12 May 1986
