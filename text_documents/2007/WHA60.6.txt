 
 
 
SIXTIETH WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY WHA60.6 Agenda item 15.6 21 May 2007 
Assessment of new Member 
 
The Sixtieth World Health Assembly,  
Having considered the report on the assessment of a new Member,1 
WELCOMES the Republic of Montenegro as a new Member to WHO and sets its assessment 
payable of US$ 1490 for 2006 and US$ 4470 for 2007, being a scale of assessment of 0.001% . 
 
Ninth plenary meeting, 21 May 2007
