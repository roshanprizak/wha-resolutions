WHA24.7  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Twenty-fourth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  nominations  of the General  Committee,1 
ELECTS  the  following  as Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the  Executive  Board  : 
Denmark,  Ecuador,  Italy,  Lesotho,  Syria,  Thailand,  Trinidad  and  Tobago,  and  Uruguay. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 4.2.1 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  12  May  1971
