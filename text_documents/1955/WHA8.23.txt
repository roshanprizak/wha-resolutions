WHA8.23  Implementation  of Resolution  WHA7.33 
The Eighth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Noting  the  report  of the  Director-General  on the  implementation  of resolution  WHA7.33,1 
1. REAFFIRMS  the  provisions  of that  resolution  ; 
2. REGRETS  that  it was  not  fully  implemented  ; and 
3. REQUESTS  the  States  Members  in the  Eastern  Mediterranean  Region  as well  as the Director-General 
and the  Regional  Director  to continue  their  efforts  with  a view  to giving  that  resolution  full  effect. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd  ed.,  5.2.5.4  Ninth  plenary  meeting,  26  May  1955  (section  6 of 
the third  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
