WHA29.62  Reports  of the Executive  Board  on its fifty-sixth  and  fifty-seventh  sessions 
The Twenty-ninth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  reviewed  the reports  of the Executive  Board  on its fifty-sixth  2 and  fifty-seventh3  sessions; 
Recognizing  the progressive  trend  towards  a more  effective  role  of the Executive  Board  in policy-making 
and assistance  to the work  of the World  Health  Assembly; 
1. COMMENDS  the Board  on the work  it has  performed  ； 
2. REQUESTS  the President  of the Twenty-ninth  World  Health  Assembly  to convey  the thanks  of the Assembly 
to those  members  of the Executive  Board  who  will  be completing  their  terms  of office  immediately  after  the 
closure  of the current  session  of the Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  I，4.2.5.2 Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  19 May  1976
