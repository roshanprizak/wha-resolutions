WHA10.3  Award  of the  Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Tenth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the  reports  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee;  1 
2. ENDORSES  the unanimous  proposal  of the Committee  for the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation 
Medal  and  Prize  for 1957; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Professor  Marcin  Kacprzak;  and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Professor  Kacprzak  for  his outstanding  contribution  and  practical  achievements 
in the field  of organization  of public-health  services  and  of social  medicine. 
Fifth  plenary  meeting,  9 May  1957
