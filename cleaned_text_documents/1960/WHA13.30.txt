The Thirteenth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that Mr Uno Brunskog be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the three financial years 1961 to 1963 inclusive, to make his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations, with the provision that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to actin his absence. Handb. Res., 5th d., 7.1.10.1 Tenth plenary meeting, 17 May 1960 (section 1 of the third report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
