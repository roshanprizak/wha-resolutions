WHA6.16  Standardization  of Laboratory  Tests  of Foods 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. EXPRESSES  the view  that  the increasing  use of various  chemical  substances  in the food  industry  has  in 
the last  few  decades  presented  a new  public-health  problem  and  might  be usefully  investigated  ; and 
2. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to study  this  subject  at its thirteenth  session. 
(Fourth  report  of the Committee  on  Programme  and 
Budget,  adopted  at the ninth  plenary  meeting,  20 May 
1953)
