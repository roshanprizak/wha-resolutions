WHA5.6  Award  of the  Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the report  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee  ;6 
2. ENDORSES  the unanimous  proposal  of this  committee  for the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation Medal  and  Prize  for 1952  ; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Professor  Charles-Edward  Amory  Winslow  ; and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Professor  Winslow  for his outstanding  contribution  to the progress  of social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  1st éd., 9.1.2.II  (Adopted  at the seventh  plenary  meeting,  12 May  1952)
