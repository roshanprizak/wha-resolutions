WHA8.4  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a Person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Eighth  World  Health  Assembly, 
After  consideration  of the  nominations  of the  General  Committee,2 
ELECTS  the following  Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board  : 
Argentina,  Ecuador,  Finland,  Pakistan,  the  Republic  of the  Philippines  and  Portugal. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd  ed.,  4.2.2  Sixth  plenary  meeting,  17  May  1955
