FIFTY-SECOND WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY WHA52.8
Agenda item 15 24 May 1999
Appointment of the External Auditor
The Fifty-second World Health Assembly
RESOLVES that the Auditor-General of the Republic of South Africa be appointed External Auditor of
the ac counts of the World Health Organization for the financial periods 2000-2001 and 2002-2003 and that he
conduct  his audits in accordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations ,
provided that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to act in his absence.
Ninth plenary meeting, 24 May 1999
