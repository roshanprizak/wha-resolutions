The Tenth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that Mr Uno Brunskog be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the three financial years 1958 to 1960 inclusive, to make his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations, with the provision that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to actin his absence. Ninth plenary meeting, 16 May 1957 (section 8 of the second report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
