The Twenty-third World Health Assembly 1. NOTES the reports of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Committee;1 2. ENDORSES the unanimous proposal of the Committee for the award of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Medal and Prize for 1970; 3. AWARDS the Medal and Prize to Dr Sabih Al-Wahbi and 4. PAYS TRIBUTE to Dr Sabih Al-Wahbi for his most significant contribution to public health in the geographical area in which Dr A. T. Shousha served the World Health Organization. Handb. Res., 10th ed.9.1.3.2 Tenth plenary meeting, 14 May 1970
