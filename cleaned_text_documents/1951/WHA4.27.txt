The Fourth World Health Assembly REQUESTS that in the future special attention should be given by the Executive Board and the Director-General to the importance of assisting Member States, particularly under-developed States, to draw up short- and long-term health programmes for their respective territories, in order to promote the orderly development of public-health measures and to utilize to the best advantage, along with the national resources, the help that may become available from time to time from WHO and other sources. (Third report of the Committee on Programme, adopted at the eleventh plenary meeting, 25 May 1951 ) [A4 / R / 83]
