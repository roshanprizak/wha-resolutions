WHA6.58  Duration  of Health  Assembly  Sessions 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. ENDORSES  the  views  on the  duration  of sessions  of the  Health  Assembly  expressed  in resolution 
EB11.R68  ; and  accordingly 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to continue  to prepare  and  organize  the sessions  of the Health  Assembly 
with a view  to limiting  their  duration  to a reasonable  minimum  compatible  with  the agenda  of the session. 
(Report  of the  General  Committee,  adopted  at the 
eleventh  plenary  meeting,  22 May  1953)
