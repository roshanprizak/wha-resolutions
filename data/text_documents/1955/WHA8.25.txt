WHA8.25 Place  of the Ninth  World  Health  Assembly 
The Eighth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  provision  of Article  14 of the  Constitution  with  regard  to the  selection  of 
the country  or region  in which  the  next  Health  Assembly  will  be held, 
DECIDES  that  the  Ninth  World  Health  Assembly  shall  be held  in Switzerland. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd  ed., 4.1.3  Ninth  plenary  meeting,  26 May  1955  (section  8 of 
the third  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
