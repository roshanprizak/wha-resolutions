The Third World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Joint OIHP / WHO Study-Group on African Rickettsioses on its first session 23 and the resolution taken thereon by the ad hoc committee of the Executive Board 1. THANKS the experts for their work, and 2. REQUESTS the Director-General to take such action as maybe necessary to implement this resolution. (Fourth report of the Committee on Programme, adopted at seventh plenary meeting, 25 May 1950) [A3 / R / 66]
