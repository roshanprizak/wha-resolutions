The Twenty-first World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Canada be appointed as member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of Mongolia be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 9th ed., 7.2.7.2 Sixteenth plenary meeting, 22 May 1968 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, second report)
