WHA13.5  Admission  of New  Associate  Members:  Central  African  Republic 
The Thirteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  the Central  African  Republic  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization, 
subject  to notice  being  given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of the Central  African 
Republic  in accordance  with  Rules  111  and  112  of the Rules  of Procedure  of the World  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th  ed.,  6.2.1.2 Third  plenary  meeting,  4 May  1960
