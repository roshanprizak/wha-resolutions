THIRTY-SIXTH  WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY WHA36.10 
10 May 1983 
APPOINTMENT  OF THE EXTERNAL  AUDITOR 
The Thirty-sixth  World Health Assembly 
1. RESOLVES  that the holder of the office of Comptroller  and Auditor  General  of the 
United Kingdom  of Great Britain  and Northern  Ireland  be appointed  External  Auditor  of the 
accounts  of the World Health Organization  for the financial  periods  1984-1985  and 1986-1987 
and that he conduct  his audits in accordance  with the principles  incorporated  in Article  XII 
of the Financial  Regulations,  with the provision  that, should the necessity  arise, he may 
designate  a representative  to act in his absence; 
2. EXPRESSES  it thanks to Mr Gordon Downey for the work he has performed  for the 
Organization  in his audit of the accounts  for the financial  period 1980-1981. 
Tenth plenary  meeting,  10 May 1983
