The Sixth World Health Assembly, Having considered resolution EB11.R58 of the eleventh session of the Executive Board concerning a campaign against smallpox ; In view of the many economic, social and other technical factors that must be considered, REQUESTS the Executive Board : (1) to proceed with a detailed study of the means of implementing such a campaign, this study to include, inter alia, consultation with Member States and with WHO regional committees, and (2) to report to the Seventh World Health Assembly. (Fourth report of the Committee on Programme and Budget, adopted at the ninth plenary meeting, 20 May 1953)
