WHA6.41  Place  of the Seventh  World  Health  Assembly 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the provisions  of Article  14 of the Constitution  with  regard  to the selection  of the 
country  or region  in which  the next  Health  Assembly  will  be held, 
DECIDES  that  the  Seventh  World  Health  Assembly  shall  be held  in Switzerland. 
(Second  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at  the  ninth 
plenary  meeting,  20 May  1953)
