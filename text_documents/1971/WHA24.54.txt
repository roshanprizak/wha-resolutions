WHA24.54  Co-ordination  with  the  United  Nations,  the  specialized  agencies  and  the  International  Atomic Energy  Agency  on administrative,  budgetary  and  financial  matters  1 
The Twenty-fourth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  resolutions  EB47.R43,  EB47.R54,  EB47.R55,  EB47.R56,  EB47.R57  and  EB47.R58 
relating  to co-ordination  with  the  United  Nations,  the  specialized  agencies  and  the  International  Atomic 
Energy  Agency  on administrative,  budgetary  and  financial  matters;  and 
Having  heard  the reports  of the representative  of the Executive  Board  and  the Director-General  on the 
subject, 
AGREES  with  the observations  and  conclusions  of the Executive  Board  on this  subject. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 8.1.1.4 Seventeenth  plenary  meeting,  20  May  1971 
(Committee  B,  fifth  report)
