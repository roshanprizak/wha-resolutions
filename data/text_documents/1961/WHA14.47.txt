WHA14.47  Relations  with  the  League  of Arab  States 
The Fourteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  Articles  50 (d) and  70 of the Constitution, 
1. APPROVES  the  proposed  agreement  to be concluded  between  the  World  Health  Organization  and 
the League  of Arab  States;1  and 
2. NOTES  the letters  exchanged  between  the  Director-General  of WHO  and  the  Secretary-General  of 
the League  of Arab  States.1 
Handb.  Res.,  5th  ed.,  8.3.2  Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  23 February  1961  (section  4 
of the fifth  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
