The Sixth World Health Assembly, Considering resolution WHA6.45, on the establishment of rules and criteria for determining the assignment to regions, 1. DCIDS that, without prejudice to any questions regarding sovereignty, the countries or territories listed in document A6 / 33 36 shall be provisionally assigned to the regions indicated in this document ; and 2. REQUESTS the Director-General, after receiving the results of the studies referred to in resolution WHA6.45, to examine any objections which might be raised and to report on this matter to the World Health Assembly. (Fourth report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the tenth plenary meeting, 22 May 1953)
