The Fifth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Canada be appointed as a member, and the member of the Board designated by the Government of Iran be appointed as an alternate member, of the Staff Pension Committee, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 1st d., 7.3.9.III (Fourth report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the tenth plenary meeting, 21 May 1952)
