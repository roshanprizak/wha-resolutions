Directors The Fifteenth World Health Assembly ESTABLISHES with effect as from 1 January 1962 the salaries for the following ungraded posts : (a) for the Deputy Director-General $19 500 per annum; (b) for Assistant Directors-General and Regional Directors $17 900 per annum. Handb. Res., 6th ed., 7.3.4 Seventh plenary meeting, 16 May 1962 (section 2 of the second report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
