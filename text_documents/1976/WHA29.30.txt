WHA29.30  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Twenty-ninth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the nominations  of the General  Committee,1 
1. ELECTS  the following  as Members  entitled  to designate  A person  to serve  on the Executive  Board  : Czechoslovakia,  Fiji,  Greece,  Honduras,  Pakistan,  Peru,  Philippines,  Qatar,  United  Kingdom  of Great  Britain  and 
Northern  Ireland,  and  Zambia; 
2. REQUESTS  the Members  so elected  to pay  due  regard  to the provisions  of Article  24 of the Constitution 
when  appointing  a person  to serve  on the Board.
