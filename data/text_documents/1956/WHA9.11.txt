WHA9.11 Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a Person  to serve  on the  Executive  Board 
The Ninth  World  Health  Assembly, 
After  consideration  of the  nominations  of the  General  Committee,2 
ELECTS  the following  Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the  Executive  Board  : 
Canada,  India,  Italy,  Mexico,  Syria,  and  the United  Kingdom  of Great  Britain  and  Northern  Ireland. 
Handb.  Res.,  3rd ed., 4.2.1 Ninth  plenary  meeting,  17  May  1956
