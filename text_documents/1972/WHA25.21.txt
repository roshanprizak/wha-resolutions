WHA25.21  Admission  of a new  Associate  Member  : Papua  New  Guinea 
The Twenty-fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Papua  New  Guinea  as an Associate  Member  of the  World  Health  Organization,  subject  to 
notice  being  given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Papua  New  Guinea  in accordance 
with  Rules  115  and  116  of the  Rules  of Procedure  of the  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 6.2.1.2  Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  19 May  1972
