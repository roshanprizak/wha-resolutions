WHA6.2  Election  and  Appointment  of the Director-General 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
On the nomination  of the Executive  Board,4 
APPOINTS  Dr.  Marcolino  Gomes  Candau  as Director-General  of the World  Health  Organization. 
(Adopted  at the fifth  plenary  meeting,  11 May  1953)
