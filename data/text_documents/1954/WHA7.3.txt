WHA7.3  Award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Seventh  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the report  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee  ; 1 
2. ENDORSES  the unanimous  proposal  of the Committee  for the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation 
Medal  and  Prize  for 1954  ; 
3. AWARDS  the medal  and  prize  to Professor  Jacques  Parisot  ; and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Professor  Parisot  for his outstanding  contribution  and  practical  achievements  in the 
field of social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd  ed., 9.1.2  Adopted  at the  fifth  plenary  meeting,  6 May  1954
