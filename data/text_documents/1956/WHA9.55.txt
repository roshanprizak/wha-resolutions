WHA9.55  Approved  1956  Technical  Assistance  Programme  and  the  Financial  Situation  for  1956 
The Ninth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  report  of the  Director-General  on the  Expanded  Programme  of Technical 
Assistance  for  the  year  1956,  including  the  financial  situation  for  that  year  ;3 
Recalling  the  provisions  of resolution  WHA7.41, 
1. NOTES  with  regret  that  the  funds  presently  available  for financing  approved  Category  I projects  in 
the field  of health  fall  short  by $247  280  of the  amount  of the  project  approval  ; 
2. EXPRESSES  the hope  that  ways  will  be found  of alleviating  this  unsatisfactory  financial  situation. 
Handb.  Res.,  3rd ed., 3.4 Thirteenth  plenary  meeting,  25  May  1956  (section  3 
of the  fifth  report  of the  Committee  on  Programme 
and Budget)
