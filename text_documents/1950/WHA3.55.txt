WHA3.55  Administrative  Office  for Africa 
Whereas  there  is no regional  organization  for Africa, 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly 
APPROVES  the  establishment  of an administrative  office  at WHO  headquarters  till such  time  as a 
regional  organization  can  be established  in Africa. (Seventh  report  of the  Committee  on Programme, 
adopted  at seventh  plenary  meeting,  25 May  1950) 
[A3/R/114]
