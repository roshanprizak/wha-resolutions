Third World Health Assembly, Having reviewed the procedure adopted for the consideration of the programme and budget for 1951 in the light of the experience gained at that Assembly, REQUESTS the Executive Board to take note of the discussion in the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters152 on this subject and to put forward to the Fourth World Health Assembly proposals for an improved procedure, bearing in mind in particular the views expressed in the committee as to the desirability of establishing suitable machinery during the early part of the Assembly, (1) fora detailed examination of the budget and programme, in addition to any arrangements tobe made fora review of the broad financial aspects of the programme and budget and the total amount thereof, (2) for adequate consideration of legal and administrative questions. (Tenth report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at seventh plenary meeting, 25 May 1950) [A3 / R / 90]
