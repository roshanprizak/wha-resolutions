The Fifteenth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Canada be appointed as member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 6th ed., 7.3.7.3 Eleventh plenary meeting, 23 May 1962 (section 5 of the sixth report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
