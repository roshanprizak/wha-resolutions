WHA28.68 Appointment  of representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Twenty-eighth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the  member  of the  Executive  Board  designated  by the  Government  of Australia  be 
appointed  as a member  of the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee,  and  that  the member  of the Board  designated 
by the Government  of Mauritania  be appointed  as alternate  member,  the appointments  being  for a period 
of three  years.
