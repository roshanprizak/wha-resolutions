WHA15.6  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a Person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  nominations  of the General  Committee,1 
ELECTS  the following  Members  as Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Board  : Canada, 
Ceylon,  Colombia,  France,  Haiti,  Madagascar,  Tunisia  and  Union  of Soviet  Socialist  Republics. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th ed., 4.2.1  Seventh  plenary  meeting,  16  May  1962
