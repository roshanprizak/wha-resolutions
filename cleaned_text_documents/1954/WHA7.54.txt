The Seventh World Health Assembly, Having reviewed the Annual Report of the Director-General, on the work of WHO in 1953 1. COMMENDS the Director-General for the work performed ; and 2. APPROVES the manner in which the activities of the World Health Organization were carried forward in 1953. Handb. Res., 2nd d., 1 Adopted at the tenth plenary meeting, 20 May 1954 (section 10 of the fourth report of the Committee on Programme and Budget)
