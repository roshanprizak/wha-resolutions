Agenda item 25.1 WHA44.17

EXTERNAL  AUDITOR 
APPOINTMENT 
The Forty-fourth  World Health Assembly, 
1. RESOLVES  that the holder of the office of Comptroller  and Auditor  General  of the 
United Kingdom  of Great Britain  and Northern  Ireland  be appointed  External  Auditor  of the 
accounts  of the World Health Organization  for the financial  periods  1992-1993  and 
1994-1995  and that he conduct  his audits in accordance  with the principles  incorporated 
in Article XII of the Financial  Regulations,  provided  that, should the necessity  arise, 
he may designate  a representative  to act in his absence； 
2. EXPRESSES  its thanks to Mr John Bourn for the work he has performed  for the 
Organization  in his audit of the accounts  for the financial  periods  1986-1987  and 
1988-1989. 
Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  13 May 1991
