WHA12.51  Report  on the  Establishment  of the  Special  Fund  by the  General  Assembly  of the  United Nations  (Resolutions  1219  (XII)  and  1240  (XIII)) 
The Twelfth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  report  of the Director-General1  and  the  recommendations  of the Executive 
Board  2 on the establishment  of the Special  Fund  by the General  Assembly  of the United  Nations; 
Considering  the  responsibility  of the  World  Health  Organization  as laid  down  in its Constitution, 
1. DELEGATES  to the  Executive  Board  the  authority  to act  on behalf  of the  World  Health  Assembly 
concerning  any  question  related  to the  Special  Fund;  and 
2. AUTHORIZES  the  Director-General  to co-operate  with  the  Special  Fund  and  to enter  into  working 
arrangements  for  the provision  of services  and  the  execution  of health  projects. 
Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  28  May  1959  (section  4 of 
the fourth  report  of the  Committee  on  Programme 
and Budget)
