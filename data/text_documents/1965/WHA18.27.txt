WHA18.27  Appointment  of Representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Eighteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the  member  of the  Executive  Board  designated  by the  Government  of Mexico  be 
appointed  as member  of the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee,  and  that  the member  of the Board  designated 
by the  Government  of India  be appointed  as alternate  member,  the  appointments  being  for  a period  of 
three  years. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th  ed.,  7.3.7.3 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  17  May  1965  (Committee  on 
Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  third 
report)
