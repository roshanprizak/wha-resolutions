WHA13.15 Publications  in Russian 
The Thirteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  report  of the  Director-General  on publications  in Russian  2 submitted  in 
response  to the  request  of the  Executive  Board  (resolution  EB25.R44), 
DECIDES  to extend  the use of the Russian  language  in certain  publications  of the World  Health  Organization,  the extension  to take  place  gradually  and  in an orderly  way  over  a period  of three  years,  beginning 
in 1961. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th ed., 1.11.1 Eighth  plenary  meeting,  11 May  1960  (section  3 of the 
second  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
