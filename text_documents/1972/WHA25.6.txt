WHA25.6  Members  in arrears  in the  payment  of their  contributions  to an extent  which  may  invoke  Article  7 
of the  Constitution  : Dominican  Republic  1 
The Twenty-fifth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  proposal  of the Government  of the Dominican  Republic  for settlement  of its 
arrears  of contributions  to the Organization  and  the recommendation  of the Executive  Board  thereon, 
DECIDES  to accept  the settlement  of its arrears  proposed  by the Government  of the Dominican  Republic 
to the  effect  that  its 1971  contribution  be liquidated  and  that  its consolidated  arrears  of contributions  for 
the period  1965-1970  be paid  in four  equal  instalments  in the years  1972-1975,  subject  to the provisions  of 
Financial  Regulation  5.5  and  notwithstanding  the  provisions  of Financial  Regulation  5.6.
