2) The Fourth World Health Assembly, Considering the need for full and precise understanding of the International Sanitary Regulations (WHO Regulations No. 2) by the health administrations which are eventually to apply them, INVITES the Director-General to prepare a memorandum giving technical and legal explanations on the various chapters of WHO Regulations No. 2 so as to facilitate their understanding, adoption and application by national health administrations. (Presented by the Committee on International Sanitary Regulations and adopted at the eleventh plenary meeting, 25 May 1951) [A4 / R / 62]
