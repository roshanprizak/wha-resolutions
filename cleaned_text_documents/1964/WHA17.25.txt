The Seventeenth World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Director-General on the Malaria Eradication Special Account and the recommendations of the Executive Board 1 Bearing in mind the decision of the Eighth World Health Assembly (resolution WHA8.30) on the establishment of the Malaria Eradication Special Account; Recalling the resolutions of the Fifteenth and Sixteenth World Health Assemblies (WHA 15.34 and WHA16.17); and Recalling the decision of the Thirteenth World Health Assembly (resolution WHA 13.24) to establish a Voluntary Fund for Health Promotion which should include, as sub-accounts, any other special ccounts which maybe placed in the Fund by the Executive Board or the Health Assembly 1. DECIDES to place the Malaria Eradication Special Account as A sub-account in the Voluntary Fund for Health Promotion; and 2. DECIDES further that the provisions of paragraph III.2 (3) of resolution WHA8.30 shall continue to govern the use of the Malaria Eradication Special Account. Handb. Res.7th ed.7.1.8; 7.1.9 Tenth plenary meeting, 16 March 1964 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, second report)
