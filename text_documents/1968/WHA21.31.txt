WHA21.31 Appointment  of Representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Twenty-first  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the member  of the Executive  Board  designated  by the Government  of Canada  be appointed 
as member  of the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee,  and  that  the  member  of the Board  designated  by the 
Government  of Mongolia  be appointed  as alternate  member,  the appointments  being  for a period  of three 
years. 
Handb.  Res.,  9th ed., 7.2.7.2  Sixteenth  plenary  meeting,  22  May  1968  (Committee 
on Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  second 
report)
