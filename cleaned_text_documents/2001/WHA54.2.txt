adultlife;Recognizing the right of everyone to have access to safe and nutritious food, consistent with theright to adequate food and the fundamental right of everyone tobe free from hunger, and that everyeffort should be made with a view to achieving progressively the full realization of this right;Acknowledging the need for all sectors of society including governments, civil society, healthprofessional associations, nongovernmental organizations, commercial enterprises and internationalbodies to contribute to improved nutrition for infants and young children by using every
