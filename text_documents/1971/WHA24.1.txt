WHA24.1  Award  of the  Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Twenty-fourth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee;  1 
2. ENDORSES  the unanimous  proposal  of the Committee  for the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation 
Medal  and  Prize  for 1971; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Professor  Eugène  Aujaleu;  and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Professor  Eugène  Aujaleu  for his unremitting  service  and  outstanding  achievements 
in the field  of public  health  and  social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 9.1.2.2  Sixth  plenary  meeting,  6 May  1971
