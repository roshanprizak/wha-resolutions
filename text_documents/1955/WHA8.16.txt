WHA8.16  Appointment  of Representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Eighth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the  member  of the Executive  Board  designated  by the Government  of Argentina  be 
appointed  as a member  of the  WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee,  and  that  the  member  of the  Board 
designated  by the Government  of Finland  be appointed  as alternate  member,  the  appointments  being 
for a period  of three  years. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd ed., 7.2.7.3  Eighth  plenary  meeting,  23  May  1955  (section  4 of 
the second  report  of the Committee  on Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
