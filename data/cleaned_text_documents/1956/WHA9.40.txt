Ninth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Canada be appointed as a member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of Italy be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years.
