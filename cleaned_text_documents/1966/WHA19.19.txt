The Nineteenth World Health Assembly DECIDES that Mr Lars Breie be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the three financial years 1967 to 1969 inclusive, to make his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations, with the provision that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to actin his absence. Handb. Res., 8th ed., 7.1.11.1 Eleventh plenary meeting, 13 May 1966 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, third report)
