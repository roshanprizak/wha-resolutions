ASSESSMENT  OF NEW MEMBERS  AND ASSOCIATE  MEMBERS  : 
ASSESSMENT  OF MARSHALL  ISLANDS 
The Forty-fourth  World Health Assembly, 
Noting the admission  of Marshall  Islands  to membership  in the Organization; 
Recalling  that the Twenty-second  World Health Assembly,  in resolution  WHA22.6, 
decided that from 1968 new Members  shall be assessed  in accordance  with the practice 
followed by the United Nations  in assessing  new Members  for their year of admission;
