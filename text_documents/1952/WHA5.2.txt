WHA5.2  Admission  to Membership  of the World  Health  Organization  : United  Kingdom  of Libya 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  the United  Kingdom  of Libya  as a Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to the deposit  of an instrument  of acceptance  of the Constitution  in accordance  with  Article  79. 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed., 6.3.1  (Adopted  at the fourth  plenary  meeting,  6 May  1952)
