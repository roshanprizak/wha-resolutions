The Forty-fourth World Health Assembly, 1. RESOLVES that the holder of the office of Comptroller and Auditor General of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the financial periods 1992-1993 and 1994-1995 and that he conduct his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations, provided that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to actin his absence 2. EXPRESSES its thanks to Mr John Bourn for the work he has performed for the Organization in his audit of the accounts for the financial periods 1986-1987 and 1988-1989.
