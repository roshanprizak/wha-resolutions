The Seventeenth World Health Assembly ADMITS Northern Rhodesia as an Associate Member of the World Health Organization, subject to notice being given of acceptance of associate membership on behalf of Northern Rhodesia inaccordance with Rules 115 and 116 of the Rules of Procedure of the Health Assembly.
