WHA14.19  Admission  of New  Associate  Members:  Tanganyika 
The Fourteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Tanganyika  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice 
being  given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Tanganyika  in accordance  with  Rules  111 
and 112  of the  Rules  of Procedure  of the  World  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th  ed.,  6.2.1.2 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  20  February  1961  (section  2 
of the third  report  of the Committee  on Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
