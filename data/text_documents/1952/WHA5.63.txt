WHA5.63  Future  Organizational  Studies  by the Executive  Board 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  that,  in the interests  of the improved  efficiency  of the Organization,  the Executive  Board should  continue  to have  detailed  studies  made  of particular  aspects  of the work  of WHO, 
1. EMPHASIZES  the importance  of constant  evaluation  of the work  undertaken  by the Organization  ; and 
2. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  at its eleventh  session  to give  particular  attention  to the study  of the 
following  subjects,  which  appear  to be of immediate  importance  : 
(1) education  and  training  programme,  including  fellowships  ; 
(2) regionalization. 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed., 7.2;  for para.  2: 1.6 ; 5 (Fourth  report  of the  Committee  on  Programme and Budget,  adopted  at the  ninth  plenary  meeting, 
21 May  1952)
