WHA6.34  World  Health  Seals 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  noted  the  report  of the  Director-General  on the  sale  of World  Health  seals  and  the 
status  of the special  fund  for seals  on 30 April  1953, 
1. DECIDES  that  no part  of this  fund  shall  at this  time  be used  to finance  the regular  budget  ; and 
2. NOTES  that  a progress  report  on the sale  of the seals  and  the status  of the special  fund  will  be made  to 
the first  session  of the Executive  Board  to be held  in each  year  and  included  in its report  to the Assembly. 
(Second  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  ninth 
plenary  meeting,  20 May  1953)
