l The Second World Health Assembly NOTES (1) the activities with the United Nations and specialized agencies, in particular with regard to WHO and UNICEF participation in venereal-disease control programmes with governments in implementation of the recommendations of the Joint Committee on Health Policy, UNICEF / WHO ; (2) the full support of the programme proposals for 1950 given by the International Union against Venereal Diseases, representing non-governmental organizations in more than 40 countries. (Third report of Committee on Programme, adopted at ninth plenary meeting)
