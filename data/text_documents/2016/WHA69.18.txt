 
 
 
SIXTY-NINTH  WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY  WHA 69.18 
Agenda item 23.2 28 May 20 16 
Process for the election of the Director -General  
of the World Health Organization  
 
The Sixty-ninth  World Health Assembly,  
Having considered  the report on the process for the election of the Director -General of the 
World Health Organization,1  
DECIDES that candidates nominated by the Executive Board for the post of Director -General of 
the World Health Organiza tion shall address  the Health Assembly before the vote for appointment of 
the Director -General, on the understanding that:  
(a) statements shall be limited to a maximum of 15 minutes;  
(b) the order of statements shall be decided by lot;  
(c) there shall be n o questions and answers after statement s;  
(d) statements shall be webcast on the WHO website in all official languages.  
Eighth plenary meeting, 28 May 201 6
