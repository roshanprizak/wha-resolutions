) The Twelfth World Health Assembly, Considering that the Economic and Social Council, in resolution 689 J (XXVI), invited the World Health Organization to transmit to the Secretary-General of the United Nations its comments on the third draft of the Single Convention on Narcotic Drugs; Considering the decision taken by the Seventh World Health Assembly in its resolution WHA7.6; and Having noted resolution EB23.R22 of the Executive Board regarding the comments 1 suggested by the Director-General concerning those articles of the draft Single Convention affecting the functions of the World Health Organization in respect of the international control of narcotic drugs, REQUESTS the Director-General to transmit these comments to the Secretary-General of the United Nations. Tenth plenary meeting, 26 May 1959 (section 8 of the second report of the Committee on Programme and Budget)
