Supplementary  agenda  item  1 2  May  1994 
Rights  and  privileges  of South  Africa 
The Forty-seventh  World  Health  Assembly, 
Noting  the democratic  elections  which  took  place  in South  Africa  from  26 to 29 April  1994  with  a 
view  to the installation  of a Government  of National  Unity  to represent  the whole  population  of South 
Africa; 
Noting  further  the coming  into  force  of a new  Constitution  in South  Africa  on 27 April  1994  which 
now governs  governmental  actions  on all levels; 
Considering  the desire  of South  Africa  to participate  henceforth  in the activities  of the World  Health 
Organization  and  its Assembly  and  thereby  to fulfil  its obligations  and  assume  its rights  in accordance  with 
the Constitution  of the World  Health  Organization; 
RESCINDS  resolution  WHA17.50;  and 
DECIDES  that  all ri^its  and  privileges  associated  with  full  membership  of the  World  Health 
Organization  be granted  with  immediate  effect  to South  Africa  at the  Forty-seventh  World  Health 
Assembly. 
Second  plenary  meeting,  2 May  1994
