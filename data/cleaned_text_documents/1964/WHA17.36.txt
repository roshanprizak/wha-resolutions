The Seventeenth World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Director-General on the Medical Research Programme of WHO for 1958-1963; 1 and Having taken into account resolution EB33.R19 of the Executive Board at its thirty-third session, 1. NOTES with appreciation the volume and quality of work accomplished under this programme; 2. EXPRESSES its satisfaction with the way this programme is being conducted; and 3. REQUESTS the Director-General, keeping in mind the discussions that took place at that Assembly : (1) to continue to promote research in the fields of major public health importance; and (2) to report periodically to the Assembly the progress of the research programme. Handb. Res., 7th ed., 1.11 Eleventh plenary meeting, 17 March 1964 (Committee on Programme and Budget, third report)
