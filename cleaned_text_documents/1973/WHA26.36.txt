The Twenty-sixth World Health Assembly, Having considered the recommendation of the Executive Board on the subject of the next organizational study 1. DECIDES that the next subject of study shall be "Interrelationships between the central technical services of WHO and programmes of direct assistance to Member States"; and 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board to report on this study to the Twenty-seventh World Health Assembly. Handb. Res., Vol. I7.4 Fifteenth plenary meeting, 22 May 1973 (Committee B, third report)
