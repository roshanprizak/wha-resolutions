WHA9.5 Admission  of Tunisia  as a Member 
The Ninth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Tunisia  as a Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to the deposit  of a formal instrument  with  the  Secretary-General  of the  United  Nations  in accordance  with  Article  79 of the Constitution. 
Handb.  Res.,  3rd ed., 6.2.1.1 Fourth  plenary  meeting,  9 May  1956
