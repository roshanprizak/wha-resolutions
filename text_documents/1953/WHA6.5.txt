WHA6.5  Admission  of Nepal  to Membership  of the World  Health  Organization 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Nepal  as a Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to the  deposit  of a formal 
instrument  with  the  Secretary-General  of the  United  Nations  in accordance  with  Article  79 of the 
Constitution.  (First  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  eighth 
plenary  meeting,  15  May  1953)
