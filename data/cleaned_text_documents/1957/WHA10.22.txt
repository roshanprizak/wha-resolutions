The Tenth World Health Assembly 1. NOTES with satisfaction the resumption of active participation in the work of the Organization by the Governments of Albania, Bulgaria, Poland and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics; 1 and 2. EXPRESSES the hope that the Member States which have not sofar notified the Director-General of their decision to resume active participation in the work of the Organization will do so in the near future. Eleventh plenary meeting, 22 May 1957 (section 1 of the third report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
