WHA22.19 Inclusion  of Afghanistan  in the Eastern  Mediterranean  Region 
The Twenty-second  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  request  from  the Government  of Afghanistan  for the inclusion  of that  country 
in the  Eastern  Mediterranean  Region,1 
DECIDES  that  Afghanistan  shall  form  part  of the Eastern  Mediterranean  Region. 
Handb.  Res.,  10th  ed., 5.1.3.1  Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  23 July  1969  (Committee  on 
Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  fourth 
report)
