The Forty-seventh World Health Assembly, Noting the democratic elections which took place in South Africa from 26 to 29 April 1994 with a view to the installation of a Government of National Unity to represent the whole population of South Africa; Noting further the coming into force of anew Constitution in South Africa on 27 April 1994 which now governs governmental actions on all levels; Considering the desire of South Africa to participate henceforth in the activities of the World Health Organization and its Assembly and thereby to fulfil its obligations and assume its rights inaccordance with the Constitution of the World Health Organization; RESCINDS resolution WHA17.50; and DECIDES that all ri^its and privileges associated with full membership of the World Health Organization be granted with immediate effect to South Africa at the Forty-seventh World Health Assembly.
