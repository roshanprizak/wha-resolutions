WHA11.6  Status  of the  Publications  Revolving  Fund 
The Eleventh  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the recommendation  of the Executive  Board  relating  to the withdrawal  of funds 
not needed  in the  Publications  Revolving  Fund, 
AUTHORIZES  the transfer  of $89  000 from  the Publications  Revolving  Fund  to miscellaneous  income. 
Handb.  Res.,  4th ed., 7.1.6  Fifth  plenary  meeting,  3 June  1958  (section  2 of the 
first report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
