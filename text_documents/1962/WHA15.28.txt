WHA15.28  Selection  of the Country  in which  the Sixteenth  World  Health  Assembly  will  be held 
The Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  the provision  of Article  14 of the Constitution  with  regard  to the selection  of the country 
or region  in which  the next  Health  Assembly  will  be held, 
DECIDES  that  the  Sixteenth  World  Health  Assembly  shall  be held  in Switzerland. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th ed., 4.1.1.2  Tenth  plenary  meeting,  21  May  1962  (section  5 of 
the fifth  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
