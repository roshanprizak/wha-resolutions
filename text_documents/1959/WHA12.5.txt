WHA12.5 Financial  Report  of WHO  for  1958  and  Report  of the External  Auditor 
The Twelfth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  examined  the  Financial  Report  of the  Director-General  for  the  period  1 January  to 
31 December  1958  and  the  Report  of the External  Auditor  for the same  financial  period,  as contained 
in Official  Records  No.  93;  and 
Having  considered  the report  of the ad hoc  committee  of the Executive  Board  1 on its examination 
of these  reports, 
ACCEPTS  the  Director-General's  Financial  Report  and  the  Report  of the External  Auditor  for  the 
financial  year  1958. 
Ninth  plenary  meeting,  22  May  1959  (section  1 of 
the first  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
