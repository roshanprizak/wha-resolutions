The Sixth World Health Assembly, Having noted the report of the Executive Board on its consideration of the rights and obligations of Associate Members in regional committees 1. RESOLVES that it is premature to adopt any changes in the existing rights and obligations of Associate Members as defined in resolution WHA2.103 ; and 2. RESOLVES that the WHO regional committees shall be invited to comment on the advisability of any such changes. (Third report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the ninth plenary meeting, 20 May 1953)
