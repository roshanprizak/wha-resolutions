WHA7.16  Future  Scales  of Assessment 
The Seventh  World  Health  Assembly 
1. RECOMMENDS  that  the  Eighth  World  Health  Assembly  give  consideration  to methods  for  assessing 
Members  which  participate  in the work  of the Organization  only  for the amount  of the effective  budget 
in 1956  and  subsequent  years,  any  assessments  of the other  Members  to be over  and  above  this  amount  ; 
2. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to ascertain  the views  of the Member  States  in this  connexion,  to study 
the replies  and  to submit  a report  to the Eighth  World  Health  Assembly  ; and 
3. INVITES  the Member  States  which  have  not been  actively  participating  in the work  of the Organization 
to resume  full  participation  at the earliest  date. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd  ed.,  7.1.2.1 Adopted  at the eighth  plenary  meeting,  15 May  1954
