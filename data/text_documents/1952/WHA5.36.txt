WHA5.36  Amendment  to the 1952  Appropriation  Resolution 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  to amend  the Appropriation  Resolution  for 1952  (WHA4.73)  30  by deleting  paragraph  VI and replacing  it by the following  : 
Notwithstanding  the provisions  of the Financial  Regulations,  the Director-General  is authorized to charge  as an obligation  against  the  1952  appropriation  the  costs,  including  transportation,  of operational  supplies  and  equipment  for which  contracts  have  been  entered  into  prior  to 31 December 1952. 
See also Handb.  Res.,  1st ed., 2.1.3.ni,  p. 125 (Third  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  ninth plenary  meeting,  21 May  1952)
