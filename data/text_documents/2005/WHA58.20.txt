 WHA58.20 Amendments to the Financia l Regulations and Financial Rules 
The Fifty-eighth World Health Assembly, 
Having considered the report on amendments to the Financial Regulations and Financial Rules,1 
1. APPROVES the changes to the Financial Regula tions, to be effective as from 1 January 2006;2 
2. AUTHORIZES, as a transitional measure, that at the end of the financial period 2006-2007 any 
unliquidated obligations from the financial period 2004-2005 shall be cancelled and credited to Miscellaneous Income.
