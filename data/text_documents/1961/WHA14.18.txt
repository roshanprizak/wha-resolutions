WHA14.18  Admission  of New  Associate  Members:  Ruanda-Urundi 
The Fourteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Ruanda-Urundi  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice 
being  given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Ruanda-Urundi  in accordance  with 
Rules  111  and  112  of the Rules  of Procedure  of the World  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th  ed.,  6.2.1.2 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  20  February  1961  (section  1 
of the third  report  of the Committee  on Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
