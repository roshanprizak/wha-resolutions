The Twenty-fifth World Health Assembly, Having noted the Supplement to the Fourth Report on the World Health Situation, including the review of the special topic Organization of Hospital Services which has been prepared by the Director-General in pursuance of resolution WHA23.24, part III 1. THANKS the Director-General; 2. THANKS Members States and Associate Members for their assistance in providing material for this Supplement; 3. REQUESTS Member States and Associate Members to submit before 31 August 1972 any amendments they wish to include in this Supplement before it is finalized and 4. RECALLS the decision of the Twenty-third World Health Assembly to request the Director-General to prepare for the Twenty-seventh World Health Assembly the fifth report on the world health situation. Handb. Res., 11th ed., 1.16.4.1 Fourteenth plenary meeting, 25 May 1972 (Committee B, ninth report)
