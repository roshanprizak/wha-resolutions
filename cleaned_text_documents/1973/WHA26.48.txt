n The Twenty-sixth World Health Assembly, Noting from the report3 of the Director-General on the Real Estate Fund that the Organization may need to provide housing units for its project staff in south Sudan if arrangements cannot be concluded with the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) to provide such housing, 1. REQUESTS the Director-General to continue his negotiations with UNDP with A view to UNDP constructing and maintaining housing for the staffs of all the specialized agencies working in south Sudan and 2. AUTHORIZES the Director-General to finance the construction of housing for WHO project staff in south Sudan, should it become necessary, from any credits available in the Real Estate Fund that are not earmarked for other purposes and, as maybe required, from the allotments of the projects concerned. Handb. Res., Vol. I, 7.1.7 Fifteenth plenary meeting, 22 May 1973 (Committee B, fourth report)
