WHA2.54.  Status  of Contributions  to the Budget  for 1948 
The Second  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  in view  the  necessity  of financing  the  programmes  of the  Organization, 
and recalling  the  resolution  adopted  at the First  Health  Assembly  urging  the  prompt 
payment  of contributions,57 
AGAIN  CALLS  UPON  States  in arrears  with  their  1948  contributions  to make  payment 
thereof  without  further  delay. 
(Second  report  of Committee  on Administration  and  Finance,  adopted  at 
ninth  plenary  meeting)
