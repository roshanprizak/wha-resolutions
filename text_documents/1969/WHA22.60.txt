WHA22.60  Reports  of the Executive  Board  on its Forty-second  and  Forty-third  Sessions 
The Twenty-second  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Executive  Board  on its forty-second  1 and  forty-third  2 sessions; 
2. COMMENDS  the Board  on the work  it has performed  ； and 
3. REQUESTS  the  President  of the  Twenty-second  World  Health  Assembly  to convey  the  thanks  of the 
Assembly  to those  members  of the Executive  Board  who  will  be completing  their  terms  of office  immediately 
after  the closure  of the current  session  of the Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  10th  ed.，4.2.5.2 Fourteenth  plenary  meeting,  25 July  1969
