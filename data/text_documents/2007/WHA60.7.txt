 
 
 
SIXTIETH WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY WHA60.7 Agenda item 15.7 21 May 2007 
Appointment of the External Auditor 
 
The Sixtieth World Health Assembly, 
RESOLVES that Comptroller and Auditor-General of India be appointed External Auditor of 
the accounts of the World Health Organization for the financial periods 2008–2009 and 2010–2011 and that he audits in accordance with the principles incorporated in Regulation XIV of the Financial 
Regulations and the Appendix to the Financial Regulations, provided that, should the necessity arise, 
he may designate a representative to act in his absence. 
 
Ninth plenary meeting, 21 May 2007
