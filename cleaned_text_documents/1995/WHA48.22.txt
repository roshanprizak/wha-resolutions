The Forty-eighth World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Director-General on the status of projects financed from the Real Estate Fund and the estimated requirements of the Fund for the period 1 June 1995 to 31 May 1996; Recognizing that certain estimates must necessarily remain provisional because of the fluctuation of exchange rates, 1. AUTHORIZES the financing from the Real Estate Fund of the expenditures summarized in part III of the Director-General's report, at an estimated cost of US$ 9 295 000; 2. APPROPRIATES to the Real Estate Fund, from casual income, the sum of US$ 7 691 000. Twelfth plenary meeting, 12 May 1995 A48 / VR / 12
