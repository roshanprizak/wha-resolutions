The Fourteenth World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Director-General on the developments in activities assisted jointly with UNICEF 1. NOTES the report of the Director-General; 2. THANKS UNICEF for its support to all aspects of maternal and child health, including communicabledisease control (particularly malaria eradication), nutrition and health services benefiting mothers and children, and the training of staff for such services; and 3. EXPRESSES its satisfaction with the continuing close and effective collaboration between the two organizations. Handb. Res., 5th ed., 8.1.4.1 Eleventh plenary meeting, 22 February 1961 (section 7 of the fourth report of the Committee on Programme and Budget)
