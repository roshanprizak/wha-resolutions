The Tenth World Health Assembly, Having examined the Financial Report of the Director-General for the period 1 January to 31 December 1956 and the Report of the External Auditor for the same financial period, as contained inOfficial Records No. 78; and Having considered the report of the ad hoc committee of the Executive Boarda on its examination of these reports, ACCEPTS the Director-General's Financial Report and the Report of the External Auditor for the financial year 1956. Ninth plenary meeting, 16 May 1957 (section 1 of the second report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
