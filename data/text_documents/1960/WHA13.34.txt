WHA13.34  Appointment  of Representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Thirteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the  member  of the  Executive  Board  designated  by  the  Government  of the 
United  Kingdom  of Great  Britain  and  Northern  Ireland  be appointed  as member  of the WHO  Staff 
Pension  Committee,  and  that  the member  of the Board  designated  by the Government  of Thailand  be 
appointed  as alternate  member,  the appointment  being  for a period  of three  years. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th ed., 7.3.7.3  Tenth  plenary  meeting,  17 May  1960  (section  5 of the 
third  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
