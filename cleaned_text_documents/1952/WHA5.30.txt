The Fifth World Health Assembly APPROVES in principle the taking overby the United Nations Technical Assistance Administration of activities connected with the manufacture of antibiotics and insecticides in the future and, provided the governments concerned are agreeable, such activities hitherto undertaken by the World Health Organization, it being understood in both cases that the World Health Organization must still maintain its function of providing scientific advice in specific fields of responsibility as opposed to undertaking industrial activities. See also Handb. Res., 1st ed., 1.13.4.Ill, p. 105 ; 1.5.2.II, p. 42 (Third report of the Committee on Programme and Budget, adopted at the eighth plenary meeting, 15 May 1952)
