WHA3.28  Bovine  Tuberculosis 
In view  of the importance  of bovine  tuberculosis  as a reservoir  of human  disease,  as emphasized  by the 
WHO  Expert  Committee  on Tuberculosis,31  and 
Recognizing  the economic  importance  of this  disease  with  respect  to the world's  food  supply, 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly 
REQUESTS  the Director-General,  in collaboration  with  other  specialized  agencies  and  other  organizations, 
wherever  possible  to encourage  investigations  and  to lend  technical  assistance  to countries  upon  request  in 
the control  of bovine  tuberculosis. 
( Fourth  report  of the Committee  on Programme,  adopted 
at seventh  plenary  meeting,  25 May  1950)  [A3/R/70]
