The Twenty-fourth World Health Assembly 1. NOTES the reports of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Committee; 2 2. ENDORSES the proposal of the Committee for the award of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Medal and Prize for 1971; 3. AWARDS the Medal and Prize to Dr Chamseddine M. H. Mofidi; and 4. PAYS TRIBUTE to Dr Chamseddine M. H. Mofidi for his most significant contribution to public health in the geographical area in which Dr A. T. Shousha served the World Health Organization. Handb.
