m The Twenty-second World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Director-General on the health problems of seafarers and the health services available to them1 pursuant to resolution EB43.R23 adopted by the Executive Board at its forty-third session, 1. THANKS the Director-General for his report; and 2. REQUESTS the Director-General to assist in the establishment of pilot centres for the health of seafarers in the ports of Auckland (New Zealand) and Gdynia (Poland) as recommended in the report. Handb. Res., 10th ed., 1.7.2.2 Twelfth plenary meeting, 23 July 1969 (Committee on Programme and Budget, second report)
