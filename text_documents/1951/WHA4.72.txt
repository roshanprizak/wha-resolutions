WHA4.72  Effective  Working  Budget  for  1952 
The Fourth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the effective  working  budget  for 1952  should  be $7,677,782,  to be financed  by : 
(1) casual  income  available  for 1952, 
(2) assessments  against  the active  Members. 
(First  report  of the  Joint  Meetings  adopted  at the 
ninth  plenary  meeting,  19 May  1951)  [A4/R/17]
