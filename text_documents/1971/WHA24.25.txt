WHA24.25 Extension  of the  Regional  Office  building  for  South-East  Asia 
The Twenty-fourth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Noting  the proposal  of the Director-General1  for the construction  of an addition  to the Regional  Office 
building  for South-East  Asia  made  necessary  by the growth  of activities  of that  office, 
AUTHORIZES  this  construction  to be financed  from  the Real  Estate  Fund,  in accordance  with  the  terms 
of resolution  WHA23.14. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 5.2.3.2 Fourteenth  plenary  meeting,  15  May  1971 
(Committee  B,  third  report)
