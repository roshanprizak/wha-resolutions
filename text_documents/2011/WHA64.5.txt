transfer in addressing the challenges of pa ndemic influenza preparedness and response,

(1) to implement the Pandemic Influenza Preparedness Framework; 
(2) to support actively the wide implementa tion of the Pandemic Influenza Preparedness 
Framework, and to consider providing ad equate resources for its implementation; 
3. CALLS UPON relevant stakeholders to give pr iority to implementing the Pandemic Influenza 
Preparedness Framework;
