WHA20.4 Award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Twentieth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee;1 
2. ENDORSES  the unanimous  proposal  of the Committee  for  the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation 
Medal  and  Prize  for 1967; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Dr Fred  L. Soper;  and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Dr Fred  L. Soper  for his unremitting  service  and  outstanding  achievements  in the field 
of public  health  and  social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  8th ed., 9.1.2.2  Fifth  plenary  meeting,  10 May  1967
