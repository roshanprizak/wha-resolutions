WHA3.27  Prevention  of Importation  of Anophelines 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  noted  the opinion  of the Expert  Committees  on Malaria,28  on International  Epidemiology  and 
Quarantine,"  and  on Insecticides,30  as to the  necessity  for  obtaining  information  on the  practicability, 
efficiency  and cost  of the various  methods  of disinsection  of ships, 
1. DECIDES  that  observations  and  experiments  in connexion  with  this  problem  should  be continued,  under 
the guidance  of the Expert  Committee  on Insecticides,  until  sufficient  information  has been  obtained  to serve 
as a basis  for WHO  regulations  on the subject,  and 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to take  whatever  action  is necessary  for this  purpose. 
( Fourth  report  of the Committee  on Programme,  adopted 
at seventh  plenary  meeting,  25 May  1950)  [A3/R/69]
