WHA7.28 Rights  and  Obligations  of Associate  Members  in Regional  Committees 
The Seventh  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the comments  of regional  committees2  on the rights  and  obligations  of Associate 
Members  as defined  in resolution  WHA2.103,  which  were  made  in pursuance  of resolution  WHA6.38, 
together  with  the recommendation  of the Executive  Board  that  no change  in the existing  rights  and  obligations  should  be made  pending  further  study  of this  subject  (resolution  EB13.R29)  ; 
1. DECIDES  to make  no change  in the rights  and  obligations  of Associate  Members  at the present  time  ; and 
2. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to continue  its study  of this  question. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd  ed.,  6.1.2 Adopted  at the ninth  plenary  meeting,  18  May  1954 
(section  8 of the second  report  of the Committee  on 
Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
