WHA17.7 Admission  of Qatar  as an Associate  Member 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Qatar  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice  being  given 
of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Qatar  in accordance  with  Rules  115  and  116  of the 
Rules  of Procedure  of the Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th ed.,  6.2.1.2 Sixth  plenary  meeting,  5 March  1964
