The Fifty-fifth World Health Assembly, 1. NOTES the amendments to the Staff Rules made by the Director-General and confirmed by the Executive Board at its 109th session concerning, inter alia, contractual reform and the system for performance management and development; 2. ADOPTS the amendment proposed to Staff Regulation 4.5 for the purpose of ensuring consistency between the Staff Regulations and the Staff Rules, and that proposed by the Administration, Budget and Finance Committee; 3. DECIDES that the amendments to Staff Regulation 4.5 shall take effect on 1 July 2002. Ninth plenary meeting, 18 May 2002 A55 / VR / 9 = = =
