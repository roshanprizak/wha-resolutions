WHA20.51  WHO  Pilot  Research  Project  for International  Monitoring  of Adverse  Reactions  to Drugs 
The Twentieth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  noted  the  report  by the  Director-General;  1 and 
Recalling  resolutions  WHA18.42  and  WHA19.35  on the  monitoring  of adverse  reactions  to drugs, 
1. NOTES  with  appreciation  the  agreement  reached  between  the  Organization  and  the  Government  of the 
United  States  of America  concerning  a grant  for  the  WHO  pilot  research  project  on the  modalities  of an 
international  system  of monitoring  adverse  reactions  to drugs;  and 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to take  the necessary  measures  for that  pilot  project  to be carried  out  and to report  on its results  to the World  Health  Assembly.
