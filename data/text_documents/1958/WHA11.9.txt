WHA11.9  Financial  Report  of WHO  for  1957  and  Report  of the External  Auditor 
The Eleventh  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  examined  the Financial  Report  of the Director-General  for the period  1 January  to 31 December 1957  and  the  Report  of the External  Auditor  for  the same  financial  period,  as contained  in Official 
Records  No.  85;  and 
Having  considered  the  report  of the ad hoc  committee  of the Executive  Board  1 on its examination 
of these  reports, 
ACCEPTS  the  Director-General's  Financial  Report  and  the  Report  of the  External  Auditor  for  the 
financial  year  1957. 
Handb.  Res.,  4th  ed.,  7.1.10.3  Fifth  plenary  meeting,  3 June  1958  (section  6 of the 
first report  of  the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
