WHA13.6  Admission  of New  Associate  Members:  Republic  of the Congo 
The Thirteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  the  Republic  of the  Congo  as an Associate  Member  of the  World  Health  Organization, 
subject  to notice  being  given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of the Republic  of the 
Congo  in accordance  with  Rules  111  and  112  of the Rules  of Procedure  of the World  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th ed., 6.2.1.2 Third  plenary  meeting,  4 May  1960
