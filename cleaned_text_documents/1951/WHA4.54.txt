The Fourth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that Mr. Uno Brunskog be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the three financial years 1952 to 1954 inclusive, to make his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations, with the provision that should the necessity arise, Mr. Brunskog may designate a representative to actin his absence. (Second report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the tenth plenary meeting, 24 May 1951) [A4 / R / 31]
