of obligations  incurred  during  the period  1 January  to 31 December  1962  in accordance  with  the provisions 
of the Financial  Regulations.

to be incurred  during  the financial  year  1962  to the effective  working  budget  established  by the World 
Health  Assembly,  i.e.,  Parts  I, II, III and  IV.
