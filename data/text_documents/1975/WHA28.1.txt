WHA28.1 Admission  of a new  Member  : Tonga 
The Twenty-eighth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Tonga  as a Member  of the  World  Health  Organization,  subject  to the  deposit  of a formal 
instrument  with  the Secretary-General  of the United  Nations  in accordance  with  Article  79 of the Constitution. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  II, 6.2.1.1  Third  plenary  meeting,  14  May  1975
