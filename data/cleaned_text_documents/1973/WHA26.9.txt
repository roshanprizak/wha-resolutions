The Twenty-sixth World Health Assembly, Having examined the financial report of the Director-General for the period 1 January to 31 December 1972 and the reports of the External Auditor for the same financial period, as contained inOfficial Records No. 208; and Having considered the report of the Ad Hoc Committee of the Executive Board 1 on its examination of these reports, ACCEPTS the Director-General's financial report and the reports of the External Auditor for the financial year 1972. Handb. Res., Vol. I, 7.1.11.3 Twelfth plenary meeting, 16 May 1973 (Committee B, first report)
