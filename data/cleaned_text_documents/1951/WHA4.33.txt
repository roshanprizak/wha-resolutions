The Fourth World Health Assembly, Having examined the Director-General's proposals for the regular programme of the World Health Organization for 1952 as presented inOfficial Records No. 31, 1. CONSIDERS this programme tobe technically sound, and inharmony with the priorities setup by the Economic and Social Council26 and the general programme of work for the specific period 1952 to 1955 inclusive ;27 2. INSTRUCTS the Director-General to implement the regular programme for 1952 as modified at the joint meeting of the Committee on Programme and the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, taking into account the comments of the joint meeting and those of the Committee on Programme. (Fourth report of the Committee on Programme, adopted at the eleventh plenary meeting, 25 May 1951 ) [A4 / R / 76]
