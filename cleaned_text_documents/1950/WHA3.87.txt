The Third World Health Assembly 1. RESOLVES (1) that Mr. Uno Brunskog be appointed as external auditor of the accounts of WHO for the financial period ending 31 December 1951. Should the necessity arise, Mr. Brunskog may designate a representative to actin his absence ; (2) that in the year of the last financial period tobe audited by the External Auditor appointed under (1) above the Health Assembly shall appoint an auditor of the accounts of WHO ; and further 2.
