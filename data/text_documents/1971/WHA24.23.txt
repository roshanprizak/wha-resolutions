WHA24.23  Appropriation  to the  Real  Estate  Fund 
The Twenty-fourth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Noting  the report  of the Director-General1  on the status  of the Real  Estate  Fund  and  anticipated  needs 
for financing  from  this  fund  during  the twelve-month  period  beginning  1 June  1971, 
APPROPRIATES  to the Real  Estate  Fund,  from  casual  income,  the sum  of US $631  000. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 7.1.6.3 Fourteenth  plenary  meeting,  15  May  1971 
(Committee  B,  third  report)
