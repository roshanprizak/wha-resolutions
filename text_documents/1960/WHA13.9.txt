WHA13.9  Admission  of New  Associate  Members:  Republic  of the Upper  Volta 
The Thirteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  the Republic  of the Upper  Volta  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization, 
subject  to notice  being  given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of the Republic  of the 
Upper  Volta  in accordance  with  Rules  111  and  112  of the  Rules  of Procedure  of the  World 
Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th ed., 6.2.1.2 Third  plenary  meeting,  4 May  1960
