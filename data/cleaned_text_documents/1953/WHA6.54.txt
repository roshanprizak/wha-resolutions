The Sixth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Switzerland be appointed as member, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of the United States of America be appointed as alternate member, of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, the appoint-ments being fora period of three years. (Fifth report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the tenth plenary meeting, 22 May 1953)
