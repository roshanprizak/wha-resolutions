WHA15.7  Financial  Report  on the Accounts  of WHO  for the Year  1960  and  Report  of the External  Auditor 
The Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  examined  the Financial  Report  of the Director-General  for the period  1 January  to 31 December 
1960  and  the Report  of the External  Auditor  for the same  financial  period,  as contained  in Official  Records 
No. 109;  and 
Having  considered  the  report  of the  Executive  Board  on its examination  of these  reports,2 
ACCEPTS  the Director-General's  Financial  Report  and  the Report  of the External  Auditor  for the financial 
year 1960. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th  ed.,  7.1.11.3  Eighth  plenary  meeting,  16  May  1962  (section  1 of 
the first  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
