WHA26.34 Assignment  of the Democratic  People's  Republic  of Korea  to the South-East  Asia  Region 
The Twenty-sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  request  from  the Government  of the Democratic  People's  Republic  of Korea 
for the inclusion  of that  country  in the South-East  Asia  Region, 
RESOLVES  that  the Democratic  People's  Republic  of Korea  shall  form  part  of the South-East  Asia  Region
