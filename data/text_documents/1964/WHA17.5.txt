WHA17.5  Admission  of Nyasaland  as an Associate  Member 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Nyasaland  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice  being 
given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Nyasaland  in accordance  with  Rules  115  and  116 
of the Rules  of Procedure  of the Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th  ed.,  6.2.1.2 Sixth  plenary  meeting,  5 March  1964
