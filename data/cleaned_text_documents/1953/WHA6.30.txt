The Sixth World Health Assembly I. RESOLVES that the assessments for 195428 shall be fixed at the same scale and under the same provisions as for 1953 ; and, II. Noting the admission of Nepal as a full Member of the Organization, RESOLVES that the following addition shall be made to the scale of assessments : Nepal 10 units. (Third report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the ninth plenary meeting, 20 May 1953)
