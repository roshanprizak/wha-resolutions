WHA5.46  Temporary  Admission  of Turkey  to the European  Region 
Having  considered  the  request  of the delegation  of Turkey  38  to the effect  that  on account  of the 
conditions  prevailing  in the  Eastern  Mediterranean  Region  which  prevent  the  regular  annual  meeting of the Regional  Committee,  Turkey  wishes  to be admitted  to the European  Region  while  provisionally 
suspending  its activities  in the  Eastern  Mediterranean  Region, 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
DECIDES  to accede  to this  request. 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed.,  5.1.1 (Fifth  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  tenth plenary  meeting,  21  May  1952)
