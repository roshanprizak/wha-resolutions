WHA22.36  Proposed  Programme  and  Budget  Estimates  for 1970  : Voluntary  Fund  for Health  Promotion 
The Twenty-second  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  programmes  planned  to be financed  in 1970  from  the Voluntary  Fund  for Health 
Promotion,  as shown  in Annex  3 of Official  Records  No.  171, 
1. NOTES  that  the  programmes  are  complementary  to the  programmes  included  in the  regular  budget  of 
the Organization  ； 
2. NOTES  further  that  the programmes  conform  to the general  programme  of work  for the period  1967-19711 
and that  the research  programmes  are  in accordance  with  advice  received  by the Director-General  from  the 
Advisory  Committee  on Medical  Research  ；  and 
3. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to implement  the  programmes  planned  for  1970  to the extent  to which 
funds  become  available. 
Handb.  Res.,  10th  ed.,  2.1.7.3 Thirteenth  plenary  meeting,  24  July  1969  (Committee 
on Programme  and  Budget,  fourth  report)
