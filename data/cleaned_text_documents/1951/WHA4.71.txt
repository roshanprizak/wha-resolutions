The Fourth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the budget level for 1952 should be $9,077,782, tobe financed by : (1) casual income available for 1952 of $477,782, (2) assessments against all Members of $8,600,000. (First report of the Joint Meetings, adopted at the ninth plenary meeting, 19 May 1951) [A4 / R / 18]
