The Third World Health Assembly ADMITS Southern Rhodesia as an Associate Member of the World Health Organization, subject to notice being given of acceptance of Associate Membership on behalf of Southern Rhodesia inaccordance with Rules 98 and 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the World Health Assembly. (Second report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at fifth plenary meeting, 16 May 1950) [A3 / R / 15]
