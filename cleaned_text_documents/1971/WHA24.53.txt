t The Twenty-fourth World Health Assembly, Recalling part II of resolution WHA20.22; Considering that the United Nations General Assembly has decided in its resolution 2735 A (XXV) to continue the Joint Inspection Unit on the existing experimental basis fora further period of two years beyond 31 December 1971, and has recommended the other participating organizations in the United Nations system to take appropriate measures for the continuation of the Joint Inspection Unit on the same basis; and Noting that the United Nations General Assembly will review the question of the Joint Inspection Unit at its twenty-seventh session, taking into account, inter alia, the views of the governing bodies of the specialized agencies concerned, DECIDES that the World Health Organization shall continue to participate in the Joint Inspection Unit on the existing experimental basis fora further period of two years beyond 31 December 1971. Handb. Res., 11th ed., 8.1.1.4 Seventeenth plenary meeting, 20 May 1971 (Committee B, fifth report)
