WHA6.55  Annual  Report  of the United  Nations  Joint  Staff  Pension  Board  for 1951  and  Report  on the Second Actuarial  Valuation  of the Fund 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the annual  report  of the United  Nations  Joint  Staff  Pension  Board  for 1951,  which  was  referred 
to the  World  Health  Organization  by the Secretary-General  of the  United  Nations,  in accordance  with 
Article  35 of the Regulations  of the United  Nations  Joint  Staff  Pension  Fund  ; and 
2. NOTES  also  the report  of the United  Nations  Joint  Staff  Pension  Board  on the second  actuarial  valuation 
of the United  Nations  Joint  Staff  Pension  Fund  as of 31 December  1951. 
(Fifth  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  tenth 
plenary  meeting,  22  May  1953)
