The Nineteenth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of the Philippines be appointed a member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of Dahomey be appointed an alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 8th ed., 7.2.7.2 Thirteenth plenary meeting, 18 May 1966 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, fourth report)
