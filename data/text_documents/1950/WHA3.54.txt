WHA3.54  Western  Pacific  Regional  Organization 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly 
(Seventh  report  of the  Committee  on  Programme, adopted  at seventh  plenary  meeting,  25 May  1950) [A3/R/113] APPROVES  the establishment  of a regional  organization  for the Western  Pacific  Region  as soon  as possible 
on the understanding  that  the expenditure  will  not be greater  than  that  provided  for in the 1951  budget.
