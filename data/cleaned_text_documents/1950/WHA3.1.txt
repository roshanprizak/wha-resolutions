The Third World Health Assembly, Having considered the recommendations of the Executive Board at its fifth session regarding a general programme of work covering a specific period as required under Chapter VI, Article 28 (g), of the Constitution of WHO, 1. APPROVES in general the proposed general programme of work covering the specific period 1952-1955 and 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board further to examine the programme in the light of the discussion in the Committee on Programme 2 and the recommendations of the regional committees, and to present to the Fourth World Health Assembly a more detailed document along the same general lines as are setout inOfficial Records No. 25. (First report of the Committee on Programme, adopted at fifth plenary meeting, 16 May 1950) [A3 / R / 8]
