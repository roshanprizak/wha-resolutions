The Fifteenth World Health Assembly DECIDES that, since Jamaica and Uganda are carrying out malaria programmes, they are eligible for credits in 1962 and 1963 in the same way as those Members listed in Schedule A attached to the Appropriation Resolutions for the financial years 1962 and 1963 (resolutions WHA14.43 and WHA15.42). Handb. Res., 6th ed., 2.1 Twelfth plenary meeting, 24 May 1962 (section 2 of the seventh report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
