WHA18.25  Selection  of the Country  in which  the Nineteenth  World  Health  Assembly  will  be held 
The Eighteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  the provisions  of Article  14 of the Constitution  with  regard  to the selection  of the country 
or region  in which  the next  Health  Assembly  will  be held, 
DECIDES  that  the Nineteenth  World  Health  Assembly  shall  be held  in Switzerland. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th  ed.,  4.1.1.2 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  17  May  1965  (Committee  on 
Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  third 
report)
