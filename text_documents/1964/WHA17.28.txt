WHA17.28  Selection  of the Country  in which  the Eighteenth  World  Health  Assembly  will  be held 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  the  provision  of Article  14 of the  Constitution  with  regard  to the  selection  of the  country 
or region  in which  the  next  Health  Assembly  will  be held, 
DECIDES  that  the  Eighteenth  World  Health  Assembly  shall  be held  in Switzerland. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th ed.,  4.1.1.2  Tenth  plenary  meeting,  16  March  1964  (Committee 
on Administration,  France  and  Legal  Matters,  third 
report)
