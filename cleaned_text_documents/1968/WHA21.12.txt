The Twenty-first World Health Assembly 1. NOTES the reports of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Committee;1 2. ENDORSES the unanimous proposal of the Committee for the award of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Medal and Prize for 1968; 3. AWARDS the Medal and Prize to Professor A. M. Kamal; and 4. PAYS TRIBUTE to Professor A. M. Kamal for his most significant contribution to publIC health in the geographical area in which Dr A. T. Shousha served the World Health Organization. Handb. Res., 9th ed., 9.1.3 Twelfth plenary meeting, 15 May 1968
