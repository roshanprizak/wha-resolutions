WHA13.51  Study  of the Nature  and  Extent  of Health  Problems  of Seafarers  and  of the Health  Services 
available  to them 
The Thirteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the report  of the Director-General  on the study  of the nature  and  extent  of health 
problems  of seafarers  and  of the health  services  available  to them, 
1. NOTES  the  report  of the  Director-General;  and 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to submit  a final  report  to the Executive  Board  at its first  session  in 
1962,  and  to the Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  5th ed., 1.5.2.3  Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  19 May  1960  (section  3 of 
the fourth  report  of the  Committee  on  Programme 
and Budget)
