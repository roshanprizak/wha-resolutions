WHA4.67  Assignment  to Regions  : Morocco,  Tunisia  and  Algeria 
The Fourth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. TAKES  COGNIZANCE  of the desire  of the French  Government  that  Morocco,  Tunisia  and  the French 
departments  of Algeria  be included  within  the European  Region  ; 
2. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to study  the  proposal  of the Government  of France  and  to report 
thereon  to the Fifth  World  Health  Assembly. 
(Fourth  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  tenth 
plenary  meeting,  24 May  1951)  [A4/R/54]
