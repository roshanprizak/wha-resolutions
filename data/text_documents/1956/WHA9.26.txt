WHA9.26  Annual  Report  of the  Director-General  for  1955 
The Ninth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  reviewed  the  Annual  Report  of the  Director-General  on the  work  of WHO  in 1955,1 
1. NOTES  with  satisfaction  the  manner  in which  the  programme  was  planned  and  carried  out  during 
1955,  in accordance  with  the  established  policies  of the  Organization  ; 
2. NOTES  with  satisfaction  that  the administrative  and  financial  affairs  of the Organization,  as described 
in the  Annual  Report  of the  Director-General,  are  sound  ; and 
3. COMMENDS  the  Director-General  for  the  work  accomplished.
