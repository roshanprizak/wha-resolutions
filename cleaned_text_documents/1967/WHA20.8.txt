The Twentieth World Health Assembly, Having considered the reports of the Director-General2 and the Executive Board3 on the payment of contributions of South Africa; and Recognizing the need to continue the prudent financial policies sofar followed by the Organization, 1. DECIDES that in 1967 and future years the amount of the annually assessed contribution of South Africa to the regular budget of the Organization be placed in the appropriation section for Undistributed Reserve of the annual appropriation resolutions; and 2. REQUESTS the Director-General to report to the Executive Board and the World Health Assembly any change in the situation concerning the contributions of South Africa. Handb. Res., 8th ed., 7.1.2.2 Eighth plenary meeting, 17 May 1967 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, first report)
