The Third World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the level of the budget for 1951 be established at US $7,300 and the amounts tobe devoted to each part of the budget be approximately : Part I US $ 277 Part II US $5,888 Part III US $1,133 it being understood that the Appropriation Resolution 104 will determine the exact amounts of the various parts of the budget. (First report of the Joint Meetings, adopted at sixth plenary meeting, 19 May 1950) [A3 / R / 47]
