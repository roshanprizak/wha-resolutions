The Thirty-fifth World Health Assembly, NOTES the status, as at 6 May 1982, of the collection of assessed contributions and of advances to the Working Capital Fund, as reported by the Director-General; 2. CALLS THE ATTENTION of Members to the importance of paying their annual instalments as early as possible in the year in which they are due, in order that the approved programme can be carried out as planned; 3. URGES Members in arrears to make special efforts to liquidate their arrears during 1982; 4. REQUESTS the Director-General to communicate this resolution to Members in arrears and to draw their attention to the fact that continued delay inpayment could have serious financial implications for the Organization. Eleventh plenary meeting, 11 May 1982 A35 / VR / 1!
