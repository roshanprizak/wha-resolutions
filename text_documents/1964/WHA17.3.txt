WHA17.3  Award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee;  2 
2. ENDORS  LIS the unanimous  proposal  of the  Committee  for  the  award  of the  Léon  Bernard  Foundation 
Medal  and  Prize  for 1964; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Professor  Robert  Debré;  and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Professor  Robert  Debré  for  his outstanding  contribution  and  practical  achievements 
in the field  of social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th ed.,  9.1.2.2 Fifth  plenary  meeting,  5 March  1964
