The Third World Health Assembly, Having considered the request of the Government of the Republic of the United States of Indonesia for inclusion of Indonesia in Regional Organization Area 3 RESOLVES that the Republic of the United States of Indonesia shall from now on form part of Regional Organization Area 3, which already comprises the following countries : Afghanistan, Burma, Ceylon, India and Thailand. fFifth plenary meeting, 16 May 1950) [A3 / R / 13]
