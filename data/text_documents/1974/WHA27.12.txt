WHA27.12  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Twenty-seventh  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the nominations  of the General  Committee,2 
ELECTS  the following  as Members  entitled  to designate  A person  to serve  on the Executive  Board:  Argentina,  France,  Guatemala,  Jordan,  Mauritius,  Sri Lanka,  Union  of Soviet  Socialist  Republics,  and  Venezuela. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  I, 4.2.1 Ninth  plenary  meeting,  15 May  1974
