WHA31.21  Medium-term  programme  for mental  health 
The Thirty-first  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  studied  all the  relevant  documents  regarding  the  mental  health  programme,  and  particularly 
resolution  EB61.R28; 
1. COMMENDS  the quality  of the documents  and  of the work  done  and  also  the progress  made  in this  field; 
2. APPROVES  the content  of the said  resolution  of the Executive  Board  ； 
3. REQUESTS  the  Director-General  to continue  to keep  the  Health  Assembly  informed  of progress  with 
this programme. 
Hbk  Res.,  Vol.  II (2nd  ed.)，1.9.5 Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  19  May  1978 
(Committee  A,  second  report)
