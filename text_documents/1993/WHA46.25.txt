FORTY-SIXTH  WORLD  HEALTH  ASSEMBLY  WHA46.25 
Agenda item 29.2 12  May 1993 
SALARIES  FOR  UNGRADED  POSTS  AND  THE 
DIRECTOR-GENERAL 
The Forty-sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Noting  the recommendations  of the Executive  Board  with regard  to remuneration  of staff in the 
ungraded  posts  and of the Director-General, 
1. ESTABLISHES  the salary  for the posts  of Assistant  Directors-General  and Regional  Directors  at 
US$ 134 454 per annum  before  staff assessment，resulting  in a modified  net salary  of US$ 79 716 (dependency 
rate) or US$ 72 087 (single  rate); 
2. ESTABLISHES  the salary  for the post of Deputy  Director-General  at US$ 148 296 per annum  before 
staff assessment,  resulting  in a modified  net salary  of US$ 86 914 (dependency  rate)  or US$ 78 122 (single 
rate); 
3. ESTABLISHES  the salary  for the Director-General  at US$ 183 158 per annum  before  staff assessment, 
resulting  in a modified  net salary  of US$ 105 042 (dependency  rate)  or US$ 93 322 (single  rate); 
4. DECIDES  that these  adjustments  in remuneration  shall  come  into effect  on 1 March  1993. 
Twelfth  plenary  meeting，12  May 1993
