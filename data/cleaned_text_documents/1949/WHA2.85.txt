e The Second World Health Assembly (1) ADOPTS the policy that the World Health Organization cannot accept any invitation to appoint members to the governing bodies of health institutions or national health organizations ; (2) REQUESTS the Director-General to thank the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine for its invitation and to inform the School that, in view of the principles involved, the World Health Organization cannot accept the invitation to appoint a member to the Court of Governors of the School. (Third report of Committee on Constitutional Matters, adopted at ninth plenary meeting)
