The Second World Health Assembly ENDORSES the policy of the Executive Board as contained inOfficial Records No. 18, page v, paragraph 2 (Supplies) it being understood that the policy as laid down does not exclude points 1.2.8 and 1.2.9 on page 5 of that volume and that the provision for these two points can be included in the provisions made in connexion with item 7.6.1 (Medical Literature, Teaching Equipment and Programme Supply Services) of the same volume. (Second re-port of Joint Meetings, adopted at tenth plenary meeting)
