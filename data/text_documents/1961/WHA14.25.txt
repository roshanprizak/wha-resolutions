WHA14.25  Accommodation  for the Regional  Office  for South-East  Asia 
The Fourteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  with  satisfaction  the steady  progress  being  made  in the construction  of the new  building  for 
the Regional  Office  for South-East  Asia;3  and

by that  government  to the  Organization  in this  matter.
