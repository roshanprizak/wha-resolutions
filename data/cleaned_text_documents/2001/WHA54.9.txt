The Fifty-fourth World Health Assembly,Noting that the United Nations General Assembly, in resolution 55 / 5, established theassessment of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia at the rate of 0.026% for 2000 and of 0.020% for2001;Recalling the principle established in resolution WHA8.5, and confirmed in resolutionWHA24.12, that the latest available United Nations scale of assessments should be used as a basis fordetermining the scale of assessments tobe used by WHO,DECIDES:(1) that the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia shall be assessed at an annual rate of 0.026% forthe year 2000;(2) that its assessment for the year 2000 shall be reduced to two-twelfths of 0.026%;(3) that the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia shall be assessed at an annual rate of 0.020% forthe year 2001.Eighth plenary meeting, 21 May 2001A54 / VR / 8= = =
