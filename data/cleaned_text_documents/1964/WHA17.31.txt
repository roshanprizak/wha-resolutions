The Seventeenth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Libya be appointed as member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of Yugoslavia be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 7th ed., 7.3.7.3 Tenth plenary meeting, 16 March 1964 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, third report)
