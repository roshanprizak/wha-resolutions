The Second World Health Assembly (1) NOTES the report of the Director-General on co-ordination and (2) REQUESTS the Director-General to continue collaboration with the SecretaryGeneral of the United Nations and the Directors-General of other specialized agencies through the mechanism of the Administrative Committee on Co-ordination and its subsidiary bodies, and by appropriate representation at the meetings of other United Nations bodies. (Eighth report of Committee on Programme, adopted at tenth plenary meeting)
