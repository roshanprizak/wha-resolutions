WHA10.5  Election  of Members  Entitled  to Designate  a Person  to Serve  on the  Executive  Board 
The Tenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
After  consideration  of the nominations  of the General  Committee,1 
ELECTS  the  following  Member  States  as Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the 
Executive  Board  : 
Afghanistan,  Australia,  Egypt,  Federal  Republic  of Germany,  Liberia  and  United  States  of America. 
Ninth  plenary  meeting,  16 May  1957
