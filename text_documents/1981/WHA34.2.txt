THIRTY-FOURTH  WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY  WHA34  • 2 
15 May 1981 
STATUS OF COLLECTION  OF ASSESSED  CONTRIBUTIONS 
AND STATUS OF ADVANCES  TO THE WORKING  CAPITAL  FUND 
The Thirty-fourth  World Health Assembly, 
1. NOTES  the status,  as at 12 May 1981, of the collection  of assessed  contributions  and of 
advances  to the Working  Capital  Fund，  as reported  by the Director-General；^ 
2. CALLS  THE ATTENTION  of Members  to the importance  of paying their annual instalments  as 
early as possible  in the year in which they are due, in order that the approved  programme  can 
be carried  out as planned； 
3. URGES  Members  in arrears  to make special  efforts  to liquidate  their arrears  during 1981 ; 
4. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to communicate  this resolution  to Members  in arrears  and to 
draw their attention  to the fact that continued  delay in payment  could have serious  financial 
implications  for the Organization. 
Twelfth Plenary  Meeting,  15 May 1981
