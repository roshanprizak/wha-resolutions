WHA5.58  Exceptional  Contribution  from  the Kingdom  of Laos 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  noted  with  pleasure  the report  of the Director-General  transmitting  communications  from 
the Government  of the Kingdom  of Laos  47  in which  this  Government  has  informed  the Director-General 
of an exceptional  contribution  of 100,000  piastres  to the  World  Health  Organization, 
1. WISHES  to extend  to the Kingdom  of Laos  its deepest  appreciation  for this  gift  ; 
2. ACCEPTS  this  gift  in accordance  with  Article  57 of the Constitution.
