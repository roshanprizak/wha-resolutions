The Sixty-eighth World Health Assembly , RESOLVES that the Chairman of the Commission on Audit of the Republic of the Philippines be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization fora four-year period from 2016 to 2019 and that he audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Regulation XIV of the Financial Regulations and the Appendix to the Financial Regulations, provided that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to actin his absence .
