The Third World Health Assembly, Having considered the request of the Governments of Viet Nam, Cambodia and Laos for temporary inclusion in the South-East Asia Region, RESOLVES that the State of Viet Nam and the Kingdoms of Cambodia and Laos shall, subject to reconsideration at a later date when the Western Pacific Regional Organization shall be established, form part of Regional Organization Area 3 which already comprises the following countries : Afghanistan, Burma, Ceylon, India and Thailand. , . ,, 3 (Fifth plenary meeting, 16 May 1950) [A3 / R / 11
