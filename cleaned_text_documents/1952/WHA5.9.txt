The Fifth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the effective working budget for 1953 should be US $8,485,095, tobe financed by : (1) casual income available for 1953 ; (2) assessments against the active Members. Handb. Res., 1st ed., 2.1 (First report of the Committee on Programme and Budget, adopted at the seventh plenary meeting, 12 May 1952)
