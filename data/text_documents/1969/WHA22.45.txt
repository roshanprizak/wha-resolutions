WHA22.45  Award  of the Dr A. T. Shousha  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Twenty-second  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Dr A. T. Shousha  Foundation  Committee;1 
2. ENDORSES  the unanimous  proposal  of the Committee  for the award  of the Dr A. T. Shousha  Foundation 
Medal  and  Prize  for 1969; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to the late  Dr M. К. Afridi；  and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to the late  Dr M. К. Afridi  for his most  significant  contribution  to public  health  in the 
geographical  area  in which  Dr A. T. Shousha  served  the World  Health  Organization. 
Handb.  Res.,  10th  ed.，9.1.3.2 Fourteenth  plenary  meeting,  25 July  1969
