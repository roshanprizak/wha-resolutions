WHA24.6  Annual  Report  of the  Director-General  for  1970 
The Twenty-fourth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  reviewed  the  Report  of the Director-General  on the  work  of the World  Health  Organization 
during  1970,2 
1. NOTES  with  satisfaction  the manner  in which  the programme  was  planned  and  carried  out  in 1970,  in 
accordance  with  the established  policies  of the Organization;  and 
2. COMMENDS  the Director-General  for the work  accomplished. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 1.16.1  Tenth  plenary  meeting,  12  May  1971
