WHA11.48  WHO  Participation  in the Expanded  Programme  of Technical  Assistance 
The Eleventh  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  studied  the report  of the Director-General  on the Expanded  Programme  of Technical  Assistance  for 1959,1  as well  as resolution  EB21.R48,  adopted  by the Executive  Board  at its twenty-first  session, 
1. NOTES  the report  of the Director-General;  and 
2. ENDORSES  the opinions  expressed  by the Executive  Board  in that  resolution.
