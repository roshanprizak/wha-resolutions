WHA19.19  Appointment  of the Externa!  Auditor 
The Nineteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
DECIDES  that  Mr  Lars  Breie  be appointed  External  Auditor  of the  accounts  of the  World  Health 
Organization  for  the  three  financial  years  1967  to 1969  inclusive,  to make  his  audits  in accordance  with  the 
principles  incorporated  in Article  XII  of the  Financial  Regulations,  with  the  provision  that,  should  the 
necessity  arise,  he may  designate  a representative  to act  in his  absence. 
Handb.  Res.,  8th ed., 7.1.11.1 Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  13  May  1966  (Committee 
on Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  third 
report)
