WHA2.34.  Co-operation  with  the United  Nations  on the United  Nations  Library,  Geneva 
The Second  World  Health  Assembly 
REQUESTS  the  Director-General  to communicate  to the  Secretary-General  of the 
United  Nations  its appreciation  of the  action  taken  by the  Secretary-General  in placing 
before  the  Ninth  Session  of the  Economic  and  Social  Council  proposals  for  the  loan 
to WHO  by the  United  Nations  Library,  Geneva,  for an indefinite  period,  of certain 
medical  and  health  material  needed  by WHO. 
(Ninth  report  of Committee  on Programme,  adopted  at  tenth  plenary 
meeting)
