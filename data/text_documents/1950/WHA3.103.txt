WHA3.103  Appointment  of Member  and  Alternate  to the Staff  Pension  Committee 
Whereas  in accordance  with  a resolution  of the Second  World  Health  Assembly,149  Dr.  J. Zozaya  and 
Professor  J. Parisot  have  been  appointed  respectively  as member  and  alternate  member  of the WHO  Staff 
Pension  Committee  for a period  of one  year  only,  and 
Whereas  the rule  laid  down  by the Second  World  Health  Assembly  as regards  the one  and  two  years 
period  of membership  applied  only  when  the Staff  Pension  Committee  was  first  constituted, 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the member  of the Executive  Board  designated  by the Government  of France  be appointed as member  of the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee  and  the member  of the Board  designated  by the Government of Chile  as alternate  member  of the committee,  the appointments  being  for a period  of three  years. 
(Eighth  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at seventh  plenary 
meeting,  25  May  1950)  [A3/R/106]
