WHA15.4  Contract  of the  Director-General:  Salary  and  Allowances 
The Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Recognizing  the  increase  in the  responsibilities  of the  Director-General  resulting  from  the  growth 
of the Organization  and  the  development  of its programme, 
1. DECIDES  that  as from  1 January  1962  his salary  shall  be $24 000,  and  his representation  allowance  $10  000; 
2. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  at its thirty-first  session  to consider  the question  of whether  a housing 
allowance  or suitable  housing  should  be provided  for  the Director-General  and  to report  accordingly  to 
the Sixteenth  World  Health  Assembly;  and 
3. AUTHORIZES  the President  of the Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly  to sign  on behalf  of the Organization 
a supplemental  agreement  modifying  accordingly  paragraphs  1 and  2 of Article  II of the contract  of the 
Director-General.3
