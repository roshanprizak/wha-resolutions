WHA16.14  Appointment  of the External  Auditor 
The Sixteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  Mr Uno  Brunskog  be appointed  external  auditor  of the accounts  of the World  Health 
Organization  for the three  financial  years  1964  to 1966  inclusive,  to make  his audits  in accordance  with  the 
principles  incorporated  in Article  XII  of the Financial  Regulations,  with  the  provision  that,  should  the 
necessity  arise,  he may  designate  a representative  to act in his absence. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th ed., 7.1.11.1 Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  18 May  1963  (Committee  on 
Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  second 
report)
