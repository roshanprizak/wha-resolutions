WHA28.27  Appointment  of the External  Auditor 
The Twenty-eighth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. RESOLVES  that  Mr Lars  Lindmark  be appointed  External  Auditor  of the accounts  of the World  Health 
Organization  for the two  financial  years  1976  and  1977,  and  that  he make  his audits  in accordance  with  the 
principles  incorporated  in Article  XII  of the  Financial  Regulations  with  the  provision  that,  should  the 
necessity  arise,  he may  designate  a representative  to act in his absence  ； and 
2. EXPRESSES  its thanks  to Mr Lindmark  for the work  he has  performed  for the Organization  in his audit 
of the accounts  for the financial  year  1974. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  II，7.1.11. Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  28 May  1975 
(Committee  B,  third  report)
