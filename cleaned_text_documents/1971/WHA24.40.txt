The Twenty-fourth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Trinidad and Tobago be appointed as member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of Thailand be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 11th ed., 7.2.7.2 Sixteenth plenary meeting, 18 May 1971 (Committee B, fourth report)
