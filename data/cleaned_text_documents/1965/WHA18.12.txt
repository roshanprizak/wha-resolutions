The Eighteenth World Health Assembly, Having considered the cost estimates for the extension of the African regional office building as reported by the Director-General in connexion with the supplementary budget estimates for 1965 INVITES all the Members in the African Region to make voluntary contributions as soon as possible to the African Regional Office Building Fund towards meeting the cost of the extension of the regional office accommodation.1 Handb. Res., 7th ed., 5.2.1.2; 2.1 Eighth plenary meeting, 12 May 1965 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, first report)
