WHA25.12  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Twenty-fifth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  nominations  of the General  Committee,2 
ELECTS  the  following  as Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the  Executive  Board  : 
Afghanistan,  Colombia,  Hungary,  Indonesia,  New  Zealand,  Niger,  United  Kingdom  of Great  Britain  and 
Northern  Ireland,  and  Zaire. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 4.2.1 Ninth  plenary  meeting,  17  May  1972
