WHA8.8 Admission  of the Sudan  as an Associate  Member 
The Eighth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  the  Sudan  as an Associate  Member  of the  World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice 
being  given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of the Sudan  in accordance  with  Rules  106 
and 107  of the Rules  of Procedure.3 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd ed., 6.1.1.4  Seventh  plenary  meeting,  20 May  1955  (section  4 of the first  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
