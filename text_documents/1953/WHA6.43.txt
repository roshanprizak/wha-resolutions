WHA6.43  Reservations  to the International  Sanitary  Regulations  (WHO  Regulations  No.  2) in respect  of Overseas  and  Outlying  Territories 
Having  considered  a report  on the rejections  and  reservations  to the International  Sanitary  Regulations  (WHO  Regulations  No.  2) submitted  by governments  in respect  of their  overseas  and  outlying 
territories,34 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. ADOPTS  the report  ; 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General,  in accordance  with  the provisions  of Article  111  of the Regulations, 
to transmit  it to all governments. 
(Adopted  at the ninth  plenary  meeting,  20 May  1953)
