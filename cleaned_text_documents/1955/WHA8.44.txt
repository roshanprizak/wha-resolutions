The Eighth World Health Assembly, Noting the report of the Director-General on co-ordination with the United Nations and specialized agencies on administrative and budgetary questions, 1. BELIEVES that satisfactory progress in administrative and budgetary co-ordination and co-operation with the United Nations and with other specialized agencies is being made ; and 2. REQUESTS the Director-General to transmit to the Secretary-General of the United Nations the record of the discussions which took place in the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters at the Eighth World Health Assembly.1 Handb.
