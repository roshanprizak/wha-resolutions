WHA12.4  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a Person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Twelfth  World  Health  Assembly, 
After  consideration  of the nominations  of the  General  Committee,1 
ELECTS  the  following  Member  States  as Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on 
the Executive  Board:  Ireland,  Luxembourg,  Nepal,  Peru,  Sudan  and  Venezuela. 
Seventh  plenary  meeting,  18  May  1959
