WHA25.28  Appointment  of representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Twenty-fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the member  of the Executive  Board  designated  by the Government  of Niger  be appointed 
as member  of the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee,  and  that  the member  of the Board  designated  by the Government  of New  Zealand  be appointed  as alternate  member,  the appointments  being  for a period  of three  years. 
Handb.  Res.,  11th  ed., 7.2.7.2 Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  23  May  1972 
(Committee  B,  fifth  report)
