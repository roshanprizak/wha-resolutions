The Fourth World Health Assembly, considering that the World Health Organization was founded on principles of solidarity and universal aid, and that the recent earthquake in El Salvador had caused considerable loss of human life and rendered great numbers homeless, aswell as depriving them of many health benefits, sent to the Government and people of El Salvador its deepest sympathy and hopes fora speedy re-establishment of normal conditions, and stood in silence for one minute to pay tribute to the victims of the catastrophe. (Seventh plenary meeting, 16 May 1951)
