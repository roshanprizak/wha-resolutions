WHA14.50 Agreement  with  the International  Office  of Epizootics 
The Fourteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the principles  to govern  the relations  between  the International  Office  of Epizootics 
and the World  Health  Organization  set out in an exchange  of letters  of 4 and  8 August  1960, 
APPROVES  the exchange  of letters  establishing  the relations  between  these  two  organizations.3 
Handb.  Res.,  5th ed.,  8.3 Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  23 February  1961  (section  3 
of the  sixth  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
