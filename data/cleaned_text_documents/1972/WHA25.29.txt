1 The Twenty-fifth World Health Assembly 1. NOTES the reports of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Committee; 2. ENDORSES the unanimous proposal of the Committee for the award of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Medal and Prize for 1972; 3. AWARDS the Medal and Prize to Dr Ahmed El Halawani and 4. PAYS TRIBUTE to Dr Ahmed El Halawani for his most significant contribution to public health in the geographical area in which Dr A. T. Shousha served the World Health Organization. Handb. Res., 11th ed.9.1.3.2 Twelfth plenary meeting, 23 May 1972
