WHA5.15  Admission  of Tunisia  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Tunisia  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice  being 
given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Tunisia  in accordance  with  Rules  101  and  102 
of the Rules  of Procedure  of the World  Health  Assembly.11 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed., 6.3.2  (First  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  seventh plenary  meeting,  12 May  1952)
