WHA20.29  Appointment  of Representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Twentieth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the member  of the Executive  Board  designated  by the Government  of Sweden  be appointed 
as member  of the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee,  and  that  the  member  of the  Board  designated  by the 
Government  of Pakistan  be appointed  as alternate  member,  the  appointments  being  for a period  of three 
years. 
Handb.  Res.,  8th ed.,  7.2.7.2 Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  23  May  1967  (Committee  on 
Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  fifth  report)
