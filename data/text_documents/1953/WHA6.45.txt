WHA6.45  Assignment  to Regions 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. DECIDES 
(a) that  the  establishment  of rules  and  criteria  to be applied  for the purpose  of determining  the 
geographical  areas  to which  territories  should  be assigned  shall  be deferred  until  the results  of the 
studies  undertaken  by the United  Nations  and  other  specialized  agencies  have  become  known  ; 
(b) that,  in the meantime,  the assignment  of Member  States,  Associate  Members  or territories  shall 
be decided  upon  by the World  Health  Assembly  on the lines  hitherto  adopted  ; and  further 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to continue  to study  the possible  redelineation  of geographical  areas, 
bearing  in mind  the delineation  approved  by the First  World  Health  Assembly.35 
(Fourth  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  tenth 
plenary  meeting,  22 May  1953)
