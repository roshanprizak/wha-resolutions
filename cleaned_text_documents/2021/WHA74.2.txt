Having considered the application from the Government of Denmark on behalf of the Faroe Islands for admission of the Faroe Islands to associate membership of the World Health Organization 1 Document A74/30. 2 Document A74/4; see also documents A74/INF./5 and A74/33. 4 SEVENTY-FOURTH WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY ADMITS the Faroe Islands as an Associate Member of the World Health Organization, subject to notice being given of acceptance of associate membership on behalf of the Faroe Islands in accordance with Rules 117 and 118 of the Rules of Procedure of the World Health Assembly. (Fifth plenary meeting, 26 May 2021)
