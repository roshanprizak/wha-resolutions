WHA17.38  General  Programme  of Work  covering  a Specific  Period 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  recommendation  of the Executive  Board  in its resolution  EB32.R13, 
1. CONCURS  in the  suggestion  of the  Executive  Board  that  the  third  general  programme  of work  for  a 
specific  period  be extended  to include  1966;  and 
2. DECIDES  to extend  the third  general  programme  of work  for a specific  period  to include  1966. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th ed., 1.1.1.2  Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  17 March  1964  (Committee 
on Programme  and  Budget,  fourth  report)
