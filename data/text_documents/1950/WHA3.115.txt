WHA3.115  Budget  Level  for  1951 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the level  of the budget  for 1951  be established  at US $7,300,000  and  the amounts  to be 
devoted  to each  part  of the budget  be approximately  : 
Part I —  US $ 277,750 
Part II —  US $5,888,500 
Part III — US $1,133,750 
it being  understood  that  the Appropriation  Resolution  104  will  determine  the exact  amounts  of the various 
parts  of the budget. (First  report  of the Joint  Meetings,  adopted  at sixth plenary  meeting,  19 May  1950)  [A3/R/47]
