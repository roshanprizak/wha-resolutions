The Fifth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the budget level for 1953 should be US $9,832,754, tobe financed by : (1) casual income available for 1953 of $852 ; (2) assessments against all Members of $8,980,200. Handb. Res., 1st ed., 2.1 (First report of the Committee on Programme and Budget, adopted at the seventh plenary meeting, 12 May 1952)
