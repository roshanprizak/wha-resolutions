WHA3.120  Election  of Members  entitled  to Designate  a Person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
After  consideration  of the nominations  of the General  Committee,170  the Third  World  Health  Assembly 
elected  the following  six members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board  :171 
Chile,  France,  Italy,  Pakistan,  El Salvador  and  Thailand. 
On the recommendation  of the General  Committee,170  the Third  World  Health  Assembly  elected  Brazil 
for a one-year  term  on the Executive  Board  to replace  Byelorussia. 
(Fifth  and  sixth  plenary  meetings,  16 and 19 May  1950)
