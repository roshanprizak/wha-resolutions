The Fourteenth World Health Assembly, Having considered the nominations of the General Committee ELECTS the following Members as Members entitled to designate a person to serve on the Board: Chile, Iceland, Iraq, Israel, Italy, Japan, Nigeria, Pakistan, Poland, Senegal, Spain and the United States of America, the terms of Chile and Nigeria being for one year, and the terms of Iceland and Pakistan being for two years, as determined by lot, pursuant to Article 25 of the Constitution. Handb. Res., 5th ed., 4.2.1 Eighth plenary meeting, 16 February 1961
