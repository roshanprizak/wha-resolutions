WHA5.74  Agreement  with  the World  Meteorological  Organization 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
APPROVES  the  agreement  between  the  World  Meteorological  Organization  and  the  World  Health 
Organization,  consisting  of an exchange  of letters  between  the administrative  heads  of the two  organizations.65 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed.,  8.2.6 (Third  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  tenth 
plenary  meeting,  21 May  1952)
