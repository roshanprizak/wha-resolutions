ms The Second World Health Assembly INSTRUCTS the Executive Board, in implementing the Supplemental Operating Programme, to give major consideration to : (1) those items transferred from the Regular Operating Programme and such part of the administrative provisions therefor as maybe appropriate ; (2) strengthening or augmenting technical services ; (3) technical training of medical and auxiliary personnel ; (4) malaria, maternal and child health, environmental sanitation, venereal diseases, tuberculosis and nutrition ; (5) programme supply advisory services. (Third report of Joint Meetings, adopted at tenth plenary meeting)
