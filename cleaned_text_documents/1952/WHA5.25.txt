The Fifth World Health Assembly, Noting resolution EB9.R35 of the Executive Board ; 21 Having reviewed the general programme of work covering a specific period prepared by the Board at its seventh session 1. CONSIDERS that this programme of work provides abroad general policy as an appropriate framework for the development of detailed annual programmes in the period 1953-1956 ; and 2. REQUESTS the Director-General to recommend to regional committees that their annual programmes be so planned as to secure adequate integration of such programmes in the approved general programme of the Organization. Handb. Res., 1st ed., 1.10 (Second report of the Committee on Programme and Budget, adopted at the eighth plenary meeting, 15 May 1952)
