WHA21.14  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a Person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Twenty-first  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  nominations  of the  General  Committee,2 
ELECTS  the following  as Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board  : Belgium, 
Canada,  Chile,  Jamaica,  Lebanon,  Mongolia,  Uganda  and  the United  Kingdom  of Great  Britain  and  Northern 
Ireland. 
Handb.  Res.,  9th ed., 4.2.1  Thirteenth  plenary  meeting,  15  May  1968
