WHA26.13  Award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Twenty-sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee;1 
2. ENDORSES  the proposal  of the Committee  for the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and 
Prize  for 1973; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Dr Keizo  Nobechi;  and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Dr Keizo  Nobechi  for his outstanding  contribution  to public  health  and  social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  I, 9.1.2.2 Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  16 May  1973
