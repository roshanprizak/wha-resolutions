WHA7.12  Election  of Members  Entitled  to Designate  a Person  to Serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Seventh  World  Health  Assembly, 
After  consideration  of the  nominations  of the  General  Committee,3 
ELECTS  the following  Members  entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board  : 
Burma,  Chile,  France,  Japan,  Saudi  Arabia  and  the Union  of South  Africa. 
Handb.  Res.,  2nd  ed.,  4.2.2  Adopted  at the sixth  plenary  meeting,  14  May  1954
