WHA3.1I8  Assignment  of the Republic  of the United  States  of Indonesia  to the South-East  Asia  Region 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the request  of the Government  of the Republic  of the United  States  of Indonesia  for inclusion  of Indonesia  in Regional  Organization  Area  3,169 
RESOLVES  that  the Republic  of the United  States  of Indonesia  shall  from  now  on form  part  of Regional Organization  Area  3, which  already  comprises  the following  countries  : Afghanistan,  Burma,  Ceylon,  India 
and Thailand.  fFifth  plenary  meeting,  16 May  1950)  [A3/R/13]
