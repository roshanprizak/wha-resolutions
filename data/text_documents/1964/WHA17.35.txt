WHA17.35  Supplement  to the Second  Report  on the World  Health  Situation 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the  supplement  to the  second  report  on the  world  health  situation,  including  the  review  of the 
special  topic  "Education  and  training  of health  service  personnel",  which  has  been  prepared  by  the 
Director-General  in pursuance  of resolution  WHA  15.43,  paragraph  7; 
2. THANKS  the  Member  governments  for  their  assistance  in providing  material  for  this  supplement; 
3. REQUESTS  the Member  governments  of WHO  to submit  before  30 June  1964  any  amendments  they  wish 
to include  in this  supplement  before  it is finalized;  and 
4. REMINDS  Member  governments  of the decision  of the Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly  to request  the 
Director-General  to prepare  for the Nineteenth  World  Health  Assembly  the third  report  on the world  health 
situation.
