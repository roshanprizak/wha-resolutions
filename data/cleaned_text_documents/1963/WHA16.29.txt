s The Sixteenth World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Executive Board on its organizational study on " Measures for providing effective assistance in medical education and training to meet priority needs of the newly independent and emerging countries " prepared at the request of the Fifteenth World Health Assembly 1. THANKS the Board for the study made; and 2.
