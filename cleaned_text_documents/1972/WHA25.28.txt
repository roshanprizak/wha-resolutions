The Twenty-fifth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Niger be appointed as member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of New Zealand be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 11th ed., 7.2.7.2 Twelfth plenary meeting, 23 May 1972 (Committee B, fifth report)
