The Twenty-eighth World Health Assembly, Having examined the Financial Report of the Director-General for the period 1 January to 31 December 1974 and the report of the External Auditor for the same financial period, as contained inOfficial Records No. 222; and Having considered the report of the Ad Hoc Committee of the Executive Board2 on its examination of these reports, ACCEPTS the Director-General's financial report and the report of the External Auditor for the financial year 1974.
