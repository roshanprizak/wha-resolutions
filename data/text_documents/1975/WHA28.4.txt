WHA28.4 Award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Twenty-eighth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee;  1 
2. ENDORSES  the proposal  of the Committee  for the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
for 1975; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Professor  Boris  V. Petrovskij  ； and 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Professor  Boris  V. Petrovskij  for his outstanding  contribution  to public  health  and 
social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  II，9.1.2 Fifth  plenary  meeting,  15  May  1975
