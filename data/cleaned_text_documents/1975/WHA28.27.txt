The Twenty-eighth World Health Assembly 1. RESOLVES that Mr Lars Lindmark be appointed External Auditor of the accounts of the World Health Organization for the two financial years 1976 and 1977, and that he make his audits inaccordance with the principles incorporated in Article XII of the Financial Regulations with the provision that, should the necessity arise, he may designate a representative to actin his absence and 2. EXPRESSES its thanks to Mr Lindmark for the work he has performed for the Organization in his audit of the accounts for the financial year 1974. Handb. Res., Vol. II7.1.11. Twelfth plenary meeting, 28 May 1975 (Committee B, third report)
