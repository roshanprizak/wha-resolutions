The Forty-fifth World Health Assembly, Noting the admission of Puerto Rico to associate membership in the Organization on 7 May 1992; Recalling that the Twenty-seventh World Health Assembly, in resolution WHA27.9, decided that the assessment of Associate Members for 1975 and future years shall be 0.01%; Recalling further that the Twenty-second World Health Assembly, in resolution WHA22.6, decided that from 1968 new Members shall be assessed inaccordance with the practice followed by the United Nations in assessing new Members for their year of admission; DECIDES that the 1992 instalment of the assessment of Puerto Rico shall be reduced to one-third of 0.01%. Twelfth plenary meeting, 13 May 1992 A45 / VR / 12
