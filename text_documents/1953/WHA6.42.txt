WHA6.42  Place  of the Eighth  World  Health  Assembly  : Invitation  from  the Government  of Mexico 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  with  appreciation  the invitation  of the Government  of Mexico  to hold  the Eighth  World  Health 
Assembly  in Mexico  City  in 1955  ; 
2. REQUESTS  the  Director-General  to study  with  the  Government  of Mexico  the  implications  of this 
invitation,  including  its financial  aspects,  and  to submit  a report  to the Executive  Board  ; and,  finally, 
3. REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to report  on this  subject  to the Seventh  World  Health  Assembly. 
(Second  report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  ninth 
plenary  meeting,  20 May  1953)
