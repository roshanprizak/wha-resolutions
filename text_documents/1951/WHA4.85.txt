WHA4.85 Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Prize 
The Fourth  World  Health  Assembly,  endorsing  the resolution  of the Executive  Board  at its seventh 
session  transmitting  the proposal  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee,58  awarded  the Léon  Bernard 
Foundation  Medal  and  Prize  to Professor  René  Sand  of Belgium,  and  paid  tribute  to Professor  Sand's 
untiring  efforts  and  extremely  valuable  contribution  to social  medicine. 
(Second  plenary  meeting,  7 May  1951)
