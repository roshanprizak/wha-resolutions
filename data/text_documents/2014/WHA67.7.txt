 
 
 
SIXTY-SEVENTH  WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY  WHA 67.7 
Agenda item 13.3 24 May 20 14 
Disability  
 
The Sixty -seventh World Health Assembly,  
Having considered the World report on disability,1 the report on disability,2 and the draft 
WHO  global disability action plan 2014 –2021: better health for all people with disability,3 
1. ADOPTS the WHO global disability action plan 2014 –2021: better health for all people with 
disability;  
2. URGES Member S tates to implement the proposed actions for Member States in the 
WHO  global disability action plan 2014 –2021: better health for all people with disability, adapted to 
national priorities and specific contexts;  
3. INVITES international, regional and nationa l partners to implement the necessary actions to 
contribute to the accomplishment of the three objectives of the WHO global disability action plan 
2014–2021: better health for all people with disability;  
4. REQUESTS the Director -General:  
(1) to implement t he actions for the Secretariat in the WHO global disability action plan 
2014–2021: better health for all people with disability;  
(2) to submit reports on the progress achieved in implementing the action plan to the 
Seventieth and Seventy -fourth World Healt h Assemblies.  
 
Ninth plenary meeting, 24 May 2014
