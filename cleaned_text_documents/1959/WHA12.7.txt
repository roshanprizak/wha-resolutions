The Twelfth World Health Assembly, Having noted a report by the Executive Board on the scale of assessment; 3 and Having noted the policies established by the Eighth World Health Assembly in resolution WHA8.5, DECIDES that for 1961 and future years the scale of assessment shall be expressed in percentages. Ninth plenary meeting, 22 May 1959 (section 3 of the first report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
