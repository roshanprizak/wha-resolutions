WHA30.15  Appointment  of the External  Auditor 
The Thirtieth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. RESOLVES  that  Mr  Sven-Ivar  Ivarsson  be appointed  External  Auditor  of the accounts  of the World 
Health  Organization  for the two  financial  years  1978  and  1979  and  that  he make  his audits  in accordance 
with the principles  incorporated  in Article  XII  of the Financial  Regulations,  with  the provision  that,  should 
the necessity  arise，he  may  designate  a representative  to act in his absence  ； 
2. EXPRESSES  its appreciation  to Mr Lindmark  for the services  rendered  to the Organization  during  the years 
in which  he has served  as its External  Auditor. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  II (2nd  ed.)，7.1.11 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  12 May  1977 
(Committee  B, third  report)
