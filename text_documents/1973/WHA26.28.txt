WHA26.28  Admission  of a new  Member  : Democratic  People's  Republic  of Korea 
The Twenty-sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the application  made  by the Democratic  People's  Republic  of Korea  for admission 
to membership  of the World  Health  Organization, 
ADMITS  the Democratic  People's  Republic  of Korea  as A Member  of the World  Health  Organization, 
subject  to the deposit  of a formal  instrument  with  the Sqcretary-General  of the United  Nations  in accordance 
with  Article  79 of the  Constitution.
