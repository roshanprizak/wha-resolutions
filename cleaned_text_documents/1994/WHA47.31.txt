The Forty-seventh World Health Assembly, Noting the recommendations of the Executive Board with regard to remuneration of staff in the ungraded posts and of the Director-General, 1. ESTABLISHES the salary for the posts of Assistant Directors-General and Regional Directors at US$ 125 677 per annum before staff assessment, resulting in a modified net salary of US$ 82 586 (dependency rate) or US$ 74 721 (single rate); 2. ESTABLISHES the salary for the post of Deputy Director-General at US$ 138 759 per annum before staff assessment, resulting in a modified salary of US$ 90 043 (dependency rate) or US$ 80 922 (single rate); 3. ESTABLISHES the salary for the Director-General at US$ 171 709 per annum before staff assessment, resulting in a modified net salary of US$ 108 824 (dependency rate) or US$ 96 540 (single rate); 4. DECIDES that these adjustments in remuneration shall come into effect on 1 March 1994. Fourteenth plenary meeting, 12 May 1994 A47 / VR / 14
