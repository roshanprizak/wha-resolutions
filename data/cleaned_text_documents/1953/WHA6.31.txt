The Sixth World Health Assembly, Having considered the report of the Director-General on arrears of contributions ; Considering it desirable that Article 7 of the Constitution be invoked after a reasonable length of time and in gradual stages in respect of Members which are in arrears, RESOLVES that, if a Member is in arrears in the payment of its financial contributions to the Organization in an amount which equals or exceeds the amount of the contributions due from it for the preceding two full years at the time of the convening of the World Health Assembly in 1955, the Assembly may consider, inaccordance with Article 7 of the Constitution, whether or not the right of vote shall be granted to such a Member. (Second report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the ninth plenary meeting, 20 May 1953)
