WHA16.16  Selection  of the Country  in which  the Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly  will  be held 
The Sixteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  the provision  of Article  14 of the Constitution  with  regard  to the selection  of the country 
or region  in which  the  next  Health  Assembly  will  be held, 
DECIDES  that  the  Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly  shall  be held  in Switzerland. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th ed., 4.1.1.2  Eleventh  plenary  meeting,  18 May  1963  (Committee  on 
Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  second 
report)
