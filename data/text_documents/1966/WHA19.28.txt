WHA19.28  Selection  of the  Country  in which  the  Twentieth  World  Health  Assembly  will  be held 
The Nineteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  the  provision  of Article  14 of the  Constitution  witli  regard  to the  selection  of the  country 
or region  in which  the  next  Health  Assembly  will  be held, 
DECIDES  that  the  Twentieth  World  Health  Assembly  shall  be held  in Switzerland. 
Handb.  Res.,  8th ed.,  4.1.1.2 Thirteenth  plenary  meeting,  18 May  1966  (Committee 
on Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  fourth 
report)
