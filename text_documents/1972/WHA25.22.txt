WHA25.22  Assignment  of Bangladesh  to the South-East  Asia  Region 
The Twenty-fifth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the request  from  the Government  of Bangladesh  for the inclusion  of that  country 
in the South-East  Asia  Region, 
RESOLVES  that  Bangladesh  shall  form  part  of the South-East  Asia  Region. 
Handb.  Res.，11th  ed.，5.1.3.1 Twelfth  plenary  meeting,  23 May  1972
