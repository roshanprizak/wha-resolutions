WHA16.3  Admission  of New  Associate  Members  : Mauritius 
The Sixteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Mauritius  as an Associate  Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to notice  being 
given  of acceptance  of associate  membership  on behalf  of Mauritius  in accordance  with  Rules  115  and  116 
of the  Rules  of Procedure  of the  World  Health  Assembly. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th ed., 6.2.1.2 Sixth  plenary  meeting,  9 May  1963
