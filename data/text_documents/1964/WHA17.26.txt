WHA17.26  Legislation  to govern  Malaria  Eradication  Operations  under  the Regular  Budget 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  the  report  of the  Director-General  on the  Malaria  Eradication  Special  Account 
and the  recommendation  of the  Executive  Board;  2 
Considering  thai  pursuant  to resolution  WHA14.15  of the  Fourteenth  World  Health  Assembly  the  cost 
of the  regular  malaria  eradication  programme  is included  as an integral  part  of the  proposed  programme 
and budget  estimates  for  1965;  and 
Considering  that  the  malaria  eradication  programme  has  been  carried  out  in accordance  with  the  provisions  of resolution  WHA8.30  and  that  these  arrangements  should  continue  to govern  the  Organization's 
malaria  eradication  programme, 
DECIDES  that  the  provisions  of paragraph  III.2  (3)  of resolution  WHA8.30  shall  govern  the  malaria 
eradication  field  programme  financed  from  the  regular  budget. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th ed.,  1.4.2;  7.1.8 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  16  March  1964  ( Committee 
on Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  second 
report)
