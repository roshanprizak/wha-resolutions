WHA28.6 Financial  report  on the accounts  of WHO  for 1974  and  report  of the External  Auditor 
The Twenty-eighth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  examined  the Financial  Report  of the Director-General  for the period  1 January  to 31 December 
1974  and  the report  of the External  Auditor  for the same  financial  period,  as contained  in Official  Records 
No. 222;  and 
Having  considered  the  report  of the Ad Hoc  Committee  of the  Executive  Board2  on its examination 
of these  reports, 
ACCEPTS  the Director-General's  financial  report  and  the report  of the External  Auditor  for the financial 
year 1974.
