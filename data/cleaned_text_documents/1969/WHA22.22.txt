The Twenty-second World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Japan be appointed as member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of Upper Volta be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., 10th ed., 7.2.7.2 Twelfth plenary meeting, 23 July 1969 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, fifth report)
