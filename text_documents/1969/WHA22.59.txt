The Twenty-second  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  examined  the  report  of the  Director-General  on the  resolutions  and  decisions  of the  United 
Nations,  the  specialized  agencies,  and  the  International  Atomic  Energy  Agency  on programme  matters 
affecting  the activities  of the  World  Health  Organization, 
1. NOTES  the report; 
2. NOTES  with  approval  the  steps  taken  by the  Director-General  in pursuance  of the  above-mentioned 
resolutions  and  decisions;  and 
3. ACKNOWLEDGES  with  appreciation  the continuing  support  and  co-operation  the Organization  is receiving 
from  UNICEF  in its efforts  for the  promotion  of health. 
Handb.  Res.,  10th  ed.,  8.1.1.3;  8.1.3 Fourteenth  plenary  meeting,  25 July  1969  (Committee 
on Programme  and  Budget,  seventh  report)
