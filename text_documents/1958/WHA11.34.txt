WHAl  1.34  Appointment  of Representatives  to the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee 
The Eleventh  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  the member  of the Executive  Board  designated  by the Government  of France  be appointed as member  of the WHO  Staff  Pension  Committee,  and  that  the member  of the Board  designated  by the 
Government  of Guatemala  be appointed  as alternate  member,  the  appointment  being  for a period  of 
three  years. 
Handb.  Res.,  4th ed., 7.2.7.3 Seventh  plenary  meeting,  12 Jane  1958  (section  5 of the fourth  report  of the Committee  on Administration, Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
