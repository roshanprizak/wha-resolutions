The Twenty-eighth World Health Assembly RESOLVES that the member of the Executive Board designated by the Government of Australia be appointed as a member of the WHO Staff Pension Committee, and that the member of the Board designated by the Government of Mauritania be appointed as alternate member, the appointments being fora period of three years. Handb. Res., Vol. II7.2.7.2 Thirteenth plenary meeting, 29 May 1975 (Committee B, seventh report)
