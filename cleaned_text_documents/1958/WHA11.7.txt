The Eleventh World Health Assembly, Having considered resolution WHA10.9, adopted by the Tenth World Health Assembly, and in particular paragraph (2) of the operative part of the resolution; and Having noted that the assessment of Ghana in the United Nations scale of assessment for 1958 is 0.07 per cent., which corresponds to 8 units in the WHO scale of assessment, DECIDES that the 1958 assessment of Ghana be firmly established at 8 units. Handb. Res., 4th ed., 7.1.2.2 Fifth plenary meeting, 3 June 1958 (section 3 of the first report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters)
