WHA29.26 Use of Arabie  as a working  language  of the World  Health  Assembly  and  of the Executive  Board 
The Twenty-ninth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  resolution  EB57.R41  adopted  by the Executive  Board  at its fifty-seventh  session  on 
the basis  of a report  submitted  by the Director-General, 
1. ENDORSES  the Executive  Board's  resolution; 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to pursue  his efforts  along  the same  lines. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  I, 4.1.5;  4.2 Ninth  plenary  meeting,  13 May  1976 
(Committee  B,  second  report)
