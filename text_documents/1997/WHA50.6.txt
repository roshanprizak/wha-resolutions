FIFTIETH  WORLD  HEALTH  ASSEMBLY  WHA50.6 
Agenda  item  22.1  12  May  1997 
Transfer  of funds  from  the Executive  Board  Special 
Fund  to the Special  Account  for Disasters  and  Natural 
Catastrophes  in the Voluntary  Fund  for Health  Promotion 
The Fiftieth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. AUTHORIZES  the transfer  of the principal  of US$  100 000 of the Executive  Board  Special  Fund  to the 
Special  Account  for Disasters  and Natural  Catastrophes  in the Voluntary  Fund  for Health  Promotion,  to be used 
for emergency  humanitarian  activities; 
2. REQUESTS  the Director-General  to report  on the use of this amount  under  the Voluntary  Fund  for Health 
Promotion  in his financial  report. 
Eighth  plenary  meeting,  12 May  1997
