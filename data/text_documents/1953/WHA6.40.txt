WHA6.40  Preparatory  Study  on International  Medical  Law 
The Sixth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Considering  the suggestion  made  by the Belgian  Government  that  a preparatory  study  of the problems 
relating  to international  medical  law  and  comparative  health  legislation  should  be undertaken, 
INVITES  the Director-General,  under  the authority  of the Executive  Board,  to undertake  such  a study 
with  the  assistance  of appropriate  organizations  and  persons  and  to report  to a future  session  of the 
Executive  Board.  (Second  Report  of the Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  adopted  at the  ninth 
plenary  meeting,  20  May  1953)
