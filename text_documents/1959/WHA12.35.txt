WHA12.35  Accommodation  for  the Regional  Office  for the Western  Pacific 
The Twelfth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  considered  a report  by the Director-General1  on the accommodation  for the Regional  Office 
for the Western  Pacific, 
1. NOTES  the  report;  and 
2. EXPRESSES  its gratification  that  a building  has  been  constructed  which  provides  the  Regional  Office 
with  satisfactory  accommodation. 
Tenth  plenary  meeting,  26 May  1959  (section  6 of 
the third  report  of the Committee  on Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
