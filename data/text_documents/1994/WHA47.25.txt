FORTY-SEVENTH  WORLD  HEALTH  ASSEMBLY  WHA47.25 
Agenda  item  30 12  May  1994 
Real Estate  Fund 
The Forty-seventh  World Health Assembly, 
Having considered  the report of the Director-General  on the status of projects financed  from the Real 
Estate Fund and the estimated  requirements  of the Fund for the period 1 June 1994 to 31 May 1995; 
Recognizing  that certain estimates  must necessarily  remain provisional  because of the fluctuation  of 
exchange  rates, 
1. AUTHORIZES  the financing  from the Real Estate Fund of the e^enditures  summarized  in part III 
of the Director-General's  report, at an estimated  cost of US$ 1 965 250; 
2. APPROPRIATES  to the Real Estate Fund, from casual income, the sum of US$ 1 721 250.
