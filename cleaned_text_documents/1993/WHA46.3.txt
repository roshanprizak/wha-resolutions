The Forty-sixth World Health Assembly, I. Pursuant to Article 31 of the Constitution and Rule 109 of the Rules of Procedure of the Health Assembly; APPROVES the contract1 establishing the terms and conditions of appointment, salary and other emoluments for the post of Director-General; II.
