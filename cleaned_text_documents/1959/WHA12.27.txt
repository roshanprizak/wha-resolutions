The Twelfth World Health Assembly, Noting resolution EB23.R76 adopted by the Executive Board at its twenty-third session, 1. DECIDES to extend by one year the second general programme of work; and 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board to continue its consideration of this item and to submit to the Thirteenth World Health Assembly a proposed third general programme of work for the period 1962-65. Tenth plenary meeting, 26 May 1959 (section 11 of the second report of the Committee on Programme and Budget)
