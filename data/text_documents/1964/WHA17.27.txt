WHA17.27  Headquarters  Accommodation 
The Seventeenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Noting  the  proposed  arrangements  reported  by the Director-General  for  the further  financing  of the 
headquarters  building,1 
1. EXPRESSES  its deep  appreciation  to the Swiss  Confederation  and  to the Republic  and  Canton  of Geneva 
for this  renewed  expression  of their  hospitality  and  their  concern  for  the objectives  of the World  Health 
Organization;  and 
2. AUTHORIZES  the Director-General  to execute  the necessary  amended  agreements  with  the Confederation 
and with  the Republic  and  Canton  of Geneva  at such  time  as the necessary  legislative  approvals  have  been 
given  to the proposed  additional  loans. 
Handb.  Res.,  7th  ed.,  7.4.2.1 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  16  March  1964  (Committee 
on Administration,  Finance  and  Legal  Matters,  second 
report)
