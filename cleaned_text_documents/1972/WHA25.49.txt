The Twenty-fifth World Health Assembly, Having considered the programmes planned tobe financed in 1973 from the Voluntary Fund for Health Promotion, as shown in Annex 5 to Official Records No. 196, 1. NOTES that the programmes are complementary to the programmes included in the regular budget of the Organization 2. NOTES further that the programmes conform to the general programme of work for the period 1973-1977 1 and that the research programmes are inaccordance with advice received by the Director-General from the Advisory Committee on Medical Research; and 3. REQUESTS the Director-General to implement the programmes planned for 1973 to the extent to which funds become available. Handb. Res., 11th ed.2.1 Fourteenth plenary meeting, 25 May 1972 (Committee A, fourth report)
