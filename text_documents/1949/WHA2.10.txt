WHA2.10.  Expert  Committee  on the Unification  of Pharmacopoeias  : Reports  on the Third  and 
Fourth  Sessions 
The Second  World  Health  Assembly 
(1) NOTES  the  reports  of the  Expert  Committee  on the Unification  of Pharmacopoeias 
on its third  15 and  fourth  16 sessions  ; and 
(2) REFERS  these  reports  to the  Executive  Board  for consideration  and  action. 
(Fourth  report  of Committee  on Programme,  adopted  at ninth  plenary 
meeting)
