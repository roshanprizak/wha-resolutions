WHA5.66  Co-ordination  of Work  of the United  Nations  and  the Specialized  Agencies 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  a report67  of the Director-General  on decisions  of the General  Assembly  of the United  Nations, 
the Economic  and  Social  Council  and  its Commissions,  relating  to the co-ordination  of effort  in the various 
activities  of the United  Nations  and  the  specialized  agencies  ; 
2. EXPRESSES  its appreciation  of the degree  of co-ordination  achieved  in the field  of health  as demonstrated 
in the  report. 
Handb.  Res.,  1st ed., 8.1.1  (Fourth  report  of the  Committee  on  Programme 
and Budget,  adopted  at the  ninth  plenary  meeting, 
21 May  1952)
