The Eleventh World Health Assembly, Having considered the recommendation of the Executive Board on the subject of the next organizational study (resolution EB21.R16), DECIDES that a further study on the WHO publications programme shall be taken as the next subject for organizational study after the Board has disposed of the study on regionalization. Handb. Res., 4th ed., 7.4.6 Seventh plenary meeting, 12 June 1958 (section 3 of the fifth report of the Committee on Programme and Budget)
