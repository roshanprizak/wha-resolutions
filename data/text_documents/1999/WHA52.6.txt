FIFTY-SECOND WORLD HEALTH ASSEMBLY WHA52.6

Agreement between the World Health Organization
and the Universal Postal Union
The Fifty-second World Health Assembly,
Having considered the report on the Agre ement between the World Health Organization and the Universal
Postal Union;
Taking into consideration Article 70 of the Constitution of the World Health Organization,
APPROVES the Agreement between the World Health Organization and the Universal Postal Union.
