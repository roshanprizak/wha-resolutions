WHA5.48  Health  Assemblies  away  from  Headquarters 
The Fifth  World  Health  Assembly 
REQUESTS  the Executive  Board  to continue  its studies  on the  holding  of World  Health  Assemblies in places  other  than  headquarters,  taking  note  of the following  points  : 
(1) the  host  countries  should  co-operate  fully  with  the Organization  and  the  delegates  attending 
the session  ; 
(2) a financial  contribution  of 50 % to 75 % of the excess  costs  involved  should  be guaranteed  either 
by the host  country  or by the host  country  in co-operation  with  the Member  States  of the region 
concerned  ; 
(3) the  host  country  should  extend  an invitation  to the World  Health  Organization  at least  18 months before  the date  of the holding  of the particular  session  of the Health  Assembly.
