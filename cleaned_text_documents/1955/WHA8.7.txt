The Eighth World Health Assembly 1. NOTES the report of the Executive Board on the status of the Publications Revolving Fund ;2 2. DECIDES that the sum of $40 000 shall be withdrawn from the balance of the Fund and applied as miscellaneous income for financing the 1956 budget ; and 3. REQUESTS the Executive Board during its seventeenth session to re-examine the status of the Fund and to report on it to the Ninth World Health Assembly. Handb.
