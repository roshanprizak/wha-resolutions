The Twenty-fourth World Health Assembly, Recalling resolution WHA19.7 on the Revolving Fund for Teaching and Laboratory Equipment for Medical Education and Training, 1. CONSIDERS that the policies and conditions governing the operation of the Fund are satisfactory; 2. DECIDES that the Fund should be continued and maintained at the present level, which is US $400 000; and 3. REQUESTS the Director-General, whenever in his opinion circumstances warrant, to submit to the Executive Board proposals for possible revision of the level and financial arrangements of the Fund and of the conditions governing its use. Handb. Res., 11th ed 7.1.6.2 Eleventh plenary meeting, 13 May 1971 (Committee B, second report)
