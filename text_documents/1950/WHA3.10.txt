WHA3.10  Pharmacopoea  Internationalis 
The Third  World  Health  Assembly 
1. APPROVES  the publication  of the Pharmacopoea  Internationalis,  and 
2. RECOMMENDS  the eventual  inclusion  of its provisions  in the national  pharmacopoeias  after  the adoption 
of the said  provisions  by the authorities  responsible  for the pharmacopoeias. 
(Third  report  of the Committee  on Programme,  adopted 
at sixth  plenary  meeting,  19  May  1950)  [A3/R/26]
