The Twentieth World Health Assembly, Having noted the reports of the Director-General'2 and the Executive Board 3 on the adjustment of the 1966 assessment of Malaysia, DECIDES (1) to establish the assessment of Malaysia for 1966 at 0.11 per cent.; (2) to apply this adjustment to the contribution of Malaysia for 1967; and (3) to revise paragraph III of the Appropriation Resolution for 1967 (resolution WHA19.41) by increasing the amount under sub-paragraph (iii) by US $8700 by transfer from the Assembly Suspense Account to US$108 700 and by decreasing the assessments against Members by US$8700 to US $53 283 990. Handb. Res., 8th ed., 7.1.2.2 Eighth plenary meeting, 17 May 1967 (Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, first report)
