The Seventy-second World Health Assembly, Having considered the request of the Central African Republic in respect of its outstanding contributions up to and including 2018 of US$ 134 646; considering also the request of the Central African Republic to reschedule payment of this balance over the period 2019 2028; Noting that this request did not comply fully with the requirements of resolution WHA54.6 (2001) as to timing and procedure, 1. DECIDES to restore the Central African Republics voting privileges at the Seventy-second World Health Assembly on the following conditions: The Central African Republic shall pay its outstanding arrears of assessed contributions, totalling US$ 134 646 over 10 years from 2019 to 2028, setout below, in addition to its annual assessment; Year US$ 2019 13 465 2020 13 465 2021 13 465 2022 13 465 2023 13 465 2024 13 465 2025 13 465 2026 13 465 2027 13 465 2028 13 461 Total 134 646 2. FURTHER DECIDES that, inaccordance with Article 7 of the Constitution, the Central African Republics voting privileges shall be automatically suspended if it does not meet the requirements laid down in paragraph 1 above; WHA 72.10 2 3. REQUESTS the Director-General to report to future Health Assemblies, as appropriate, on the prevailing situation; 4. FURTHER REQUESTS the Director-General to communicate this resolution to the Government of the Central African Republic. Seventh plenary meeting, 28 May 201 9 A72 / VR / 7 = = =
