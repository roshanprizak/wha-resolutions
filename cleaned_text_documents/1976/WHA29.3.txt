The Twenty-ninth World Health Assembly, Having examined the Financial Report of the Director-General for the period 1 January to 31 December 1975 and the report of the External Auditor for the same financial period, as contained inOfficial Records No. 230; Having considered the report of the Ad Hoc Committee of the Executive Board 2 on its examination of these reports; ACCEPTS the Director-General's financial report and the report of the External Auditor for the financial year 1975.
