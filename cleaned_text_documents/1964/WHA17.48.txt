The Seventeenth World Health Assembly, Having considered the recommendation of the Executive Board in its resolution EB33.R40 on the subject of the next organizational study, 1. DECIDES that the next subject of study shall be " Co-ordination at the national level in relation to the technical co-operation field programme of the Organization "; and 2. REQUESTS the Executive Board to report on this study to the Nineteenth World Health Assembly. Handb. Res., 7th ed., 7.5 Twelfth plenary meeting, 19 March 1964 (Committee on Programme and Budget, seventh report)
