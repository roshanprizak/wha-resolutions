WHA29.3 Financial  report  on the accounts  of WHO  for 1975  and  report  of the External  Auditor 
The Twenty-ninth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  examined  the Financial  Report  of the Director-General  for the period  1 January  to 31 December 
1975  and  the  report  of the External  Auditor  for the same  financial  period,  as contained  in Official  Records 
No. 230; 
Having  considered  the  report  of the  Ad Hoc  Committee  of the  Executive  Board  2 on its examination 
of these  reports; 
ACCEPTS  the Director-General's  financial  report  and  the report  of the External  Auditor  for the financial 
year  1975. 
Handb.  Res”  Vol.  II，7.1.11.3  Eighth  plenary  meeting,  11  May  1976 (Committee  B,  first  report)
