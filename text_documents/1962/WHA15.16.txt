WHA15.16  Admission  of New  Members:  Western  Samoa 
The Fifteenth  World  Health  Assembly 
ADMITS  Western  Samoa  as a Member  of the World  Health  Organization,  subject  to the deposit  of a 
formal  instrument  with  the Secretary-General  of the United  Nations  in accordance  with  Article  79 of the Constitution. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th  ed.,  6.2.1.1  Eighth  plenary  meeting,  16 May  1962  (section  4 of 
the fourth  report  of the Committee  on Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
