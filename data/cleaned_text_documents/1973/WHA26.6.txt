The Twenty-sixth World Health Assembly 1. NOTES the reports of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Committee;2 2. ENDORSES the proposal of the Committee for the award of the Dr A. T. Shousha Foundation Medal and Prize for 1973; 3. AWARDS the Medal and Prize to Professor Mansour Haseeb; and 4. PAYS TRIBUTE to Professor Mansour Haseeb for his most significant contribution to public health in the geographical area in which Dr A. T. Shousha served the World Health Organization. Handb. Res., Vol. I, 9.1.3 Eleventh plenary meeting, 15 May 1973
