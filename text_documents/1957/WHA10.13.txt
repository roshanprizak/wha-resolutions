WHA10.13  Appointment  of External  Auditor  for  the  Years  1958-60 
The Tenth  World  Health  Assembly 
RESOLVES  that  Mr Uno  Brunskog  be appointed  External  Auditor  of the accounts  of the World  Health 
Organization  for  the three  financial  years  1958  to 1960  inclusive,  to make  his audits  in accordance  with 
the principles  incorporated  in Article  XII  of the  Financial  Regulations,  with  the  provision  that,  should 
the necessity  arise,  he may  designate  a representative  to act in his absence. 
Ninth  plenary  meeting,  16 May  1957  (section  8 of the 
second  report  of the  Committee  on  Administration, 
Finance  and  Legal  Matters)
