WHA16.12  Annual  Report  of the  Director-General  for  1962 
The Sixteenth  World  Health  Assembly, 
Having  reviewed  the  Report  of the Director-General  on the  work  of WHO  during  1962,1 
1. NOTES  with  satisfaction  the manner  in which  the programme  was  planned  and  carried  out  in 1962,  in 
accordance  with  the established  policies  of the Organization;  and 
2. COMMENDS  the Director-General  for  the  work  accomplished. 
Handb.  Res.,  6th ed., 1.14.1  Tenth  plenary  meeting,  16  May  1963
