WHA31.2  Message  of sympathy  to the Italian  Government 
The Thirty-first  World  Health  Assembly, 
Deeply  grieved  by the announcement  of the tragic  death  of Mr Aldo  Moro,  an eminent  Italian  leader 
murdered  in particularly  dramatic  and  deplorable  circumstances; 
EXPRESSES  its most  sincere  condolences  and  deep  sympathy  to his family  and  to the Italian  people. 
Hbk  Res.,  Vol.  I, 9.4 Fifth  plenary  meeting,  10 May  1978
