WHA29.51  Award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and  Prize 
The Twenty-ninth  World  Health  Assembly 
1. NOTES  the reports  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Committee;1 
2. ENDORSES  the proposal  of the Committee  for  the award  of the Léon  Bernard  Foundation  Medal  and 
Prize  for 1976; 
3. AWARDS  the Medal  and  Prize  to Professor  V. Ramalingaswami  ; 
4. PAYS  TRIBUTE  to Professor  V. Ramalingaswami  for  his outstanding  contribution  to public  health  and 
social  medicine. 
Handb.  Res.,  Vol.  II，9.1.2 Tenth  plenary  meeting,  17 May  1976
