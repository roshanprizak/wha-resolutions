The Sixth World Health Assembly, Considering that it is not yet desirable to provide for the establishment of the system of biennial Health Assemblies approved in principle by the Third World Health Assembly (resolution WHA3.96) ; Having studied all the amendments contained in resolution EB11.R69 of the Executive Board and circulated among Member States, DECIDES not to accept the proposed amendments to the Constitution for the time being, and to consider the matter again at a future Health Assembly. (Fifth report of the Committee on Administration, Finance and Legal Matters, adopted at the tenth plenary meeting, 22 May 1953)
