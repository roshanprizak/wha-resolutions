WHA4.89  Election  of Members  entitled  to designate  a Person  to serve  on the Executive  Board 
The Fourth  World  Health  Assembly, 
After  consideration  of the nominations  of the General  Committee,  elected  the following  six Members 
entitled  to designate  a person  to serve  on the Executive  Board  : 
Belgium,  Ceylon,  Cuba,  Greece,  Lebanon,  Liberia. 
(Eighth  plenary  meeting,  16 May  1951  )
